# Sporefall

Single-boss Midnight Season 1 raid added in patch **12.0.7 "Revelations"**
(live 2026-06-16). Located in the fungal valleys of **Harandar**, it pits the
raid against **Rotmire**, a fungal giant catalyzed beyond natural limits.

> Journal blurb: A fungal creature enhanced beyond natural limits — its spores
> "can catalyze the growth of surrounding fungi, sprouting new dangers for the
> unwary."

Available in **Raid Finder / Normal / Heroic / Mythic**. Weekly lockout.
Mythic is the game's first **flexible Mythic** difficulty, scaling **15–25**
players rather than a fixed 20.

| Boss | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Rotmire | Fungal giant; single encounter |

## Rotmire — mechanics

An energy/loop-style encounter: a small repeating ability list with **no
health-threshold phase transitions** — the same loop runs from pull to kill,
with a passive soft enrage forcing a kill before raid damage becomes
unhealable.

- **Fungal Bloom** — raid-wide putrid miasma: very heavy Nature damage to every
  player, applies a **16 s** Nature damage-over-time, then knocks the raid back.
- **Putrid Fist** — heavy Physical hit on the current tank, knocks the target
  upward, and stacks a debuff increasing **Physical damage taken by 75% for
  16 s**. Tanks swap on every cast.
- **Rotting Pustules** — passive, stacking raid-wide damage-over-time that never
  wears off; acts as the soft enrage.
- **Festering Vines** — roots several players in thorny vines (Nature damage per
  second, **30%** movement slow) and spawns **Shroomlings** (all difficulties),
  with **Funglings** added on Mythic.
- **Cross Fertilization** (Mythic only) — Shroomling and Fungling corpses cannot
  be within **10 yards** of each other; mismatched corpse types too close create
  a **Bursting Doomshroom** explosion. Corpse placement must be managed.

## Loot

**Sporefused gear** by difficulty:

| Difficulty | ilvl |
|------------|------|
| Raid Finder | 259 |
| Normal | 272 |
| Heroic | 285 |
| Mythic | 298 |

No tier set or class weapons drop here. RF entry requirement: level **90**,
~ilvl 240.

Notable drops:

- **Sporelord's Mycelial Insignia** (trinket) — random secondary stats + Leech.
- **Rotmire's Sporeheart** (neck) — spawns protective totems that absorb damage.
- **Sporecaller's Blooming Loop** (ring) — inflicts a Nature damage-over-time.

Collectibles:

- **Luminous Sporeglider** (mount) — earned by collecting **4 Delicious
  Sporesnacks**, one **per week per account** from a Rotmire kill on **any
  difficulty** (so four weeks minimum, difficulty doesn't matter).
- **Luminous Rotshroom** (housing decor).
- **Madcap Redcap** (toy) — 20-minute buff, 1-hour cooldown, persists through
  death.
- Additional cosmetics / achievement rewards.
