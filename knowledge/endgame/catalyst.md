# The Catalyst (Midnight Season 1)

Console in **Silvermoon City**; converts eligible gear into class tier-set
pieces at the same ilvl.

## Charges

- 1 charge at season start (2026-03-17), then **+1 every two weeks**.
- +1 bonus from achievement *Midnight Season 1: Champion of the Dawn*
  (1600 PvP rating / 2000 M+ rating / Heroic-or-Mythic raid clear).
- After you have 4pc: bonus charges can drop from raid/M+/delves/rated PvP.
- **Cap: 8 charges** per character. **No catch-up** mechanic.

## What converts

- PvE gear **Veteran track or higher**; PvP gear (Honor/Conquest/War Mode).
- **Crafted gear CANNOT be catalyzed** — never spark-craft tier slots
  (head/shoulders/chest/hands/legs) expecting to convert.
- Only head/shoulder/chest/hands/legs results count for set bonuses;
  other slots convert for appearance only. Old-season gear converts free
  (transmog only).
- Output keeps ilvl, upgrade track, tertiaries, sockets; secondary stats
  are fixed per tier piece.

## Strategy

- Catalyze **highest-ilvl, highest-track** non-tier pieces in tier slots;
  upgrade with crests *before* catalyzing only if you won't replace soon
  (track carries over, so upgrading after also works).
- Charges accrue slowly and cap at 8 — banking a few is fine, but sitting
  at cap wastes accrual.
