# Algeth'ar Academy — Midnight S1 M+

Four bosses. Three (Overgrown Ancient, Crawth, Vexamus) are open in any order;
**Echo of Doragosa unlocks only after the other three die.** Each of the first
three has an activation gate (clear its trash area / score goals / clear 3
packs). On entry, talk to one Dragonflight Recruiter in The Quad for a
dungeon-long stat buff (Bronze 5% Haste, Blue Mastery, Green +10% healing
received, Red 5% Vers, Black 5% Crit — one only, swappable).

Boss-name corroboration: guide spellings match the Blizzard journal exactly
(Vexamus 2509, Overgrown Ancient 2512, Crawth 2495, Echo of Doragosa 2514).

## Route

1. **The Quad** — land down the invisible bridge; pick up a Dragonflight stat buff.
2. **The Botanica** (up the stairs, right) — clear Vile Lasher + Hungry Lasher
   packs and Aggravated Skitterfly to activate **Overgrown Ancient**. Kill boss.
3. **Center platform** (straight on, invisible bridge) — kill the **Guardian
   Sentry** mini-boss.
4. **The Pitch** — clear 3 waves of Territorial Eagle + Alpha Eagle to activate
   **Crawth**. Score Play Ball! goals into the fire ring; kill boss.
5. **Algeth'ar Academy proper** — clear 3 packs (Corrupted Manafiend, Arcane
   Ravager/Forager, Spellbound Battleaxe/Scepter, Unruly Textbook). Professor
   Maxdormu casts Recall History to summon **Vexamus**. Kill boss.
6. **The Headteacher's Enclave** — clear Algeth'ar Echoknight + Spectral Invoker
   packs; with the first three bosses dead, pull **Echo of Doragosa**. Kill boss.

## Bosses

### Overgrown Ancient <!-- enc:2512 -->

**Hint:** cleave the lashers
Stay stacked; move together as a group during the **Germinate** channel so the
summoned Hungry Lashers spawn in cleave range. At 100 energy **Burst Forth**
awakens all surviving dormant Lashers — pre-heal and pre-mitigate.

| Ability | What it does | Do | Archetype | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Germinate | Channels to grow waves of dormant Hungry Lashers. | Move the group together so spawns cluster for cleave; kill Lashers before Burst Forth. | kill-priority-add | 🔵 | DPS |
| Burst Forth | At 100 energy, awakens all dormant Lashers + heavy party-wide damage. | Healer pre-tops, everyone pre-mitigates; tank picks up the awakened Lashers. | raid-damage | 🔴 | all |
| Hungry Lasher (Lasher Toxin) | Awakened Lashers stack a poison (Lasher Toxin) fast and hit hard if loose. | Tank grabs threat; rotate poison dispels; stun/slow/knockback to kite. | dispel | 🟠 | all |
| Barkbreaker | Tank smash that also leaves a big Physical-damage-taken amp. | Active mitigation; very dangerous if any Lashers are alive. | tank-buster | 🔵 | tank |
| Branch Out → Ancient Branch | Spawns an Ancient Branch add that casts **Healing Touch**. | Swap to it, interrupt Healing Touch; tank taunts on spawn. | kill-priority-add | 🔵 | all |
| Healing Touch | Ancient Branch heals the boss / Lashers. | Interrupt at all costs. | interruptible-cast | 🔴 | all |
| Splinterbark / Abundance | Branch applies Splinterbark bleed party-wide; on death drops **Abundance** circle. | Stand in the Abundance circle (8 yd) to cleanse Splinterbark; time the Branch death when Lashers are near dead so they don't get healed. | dispel | 🔵 | all |

### Crawth <!-- enc:2495 -->

**Hint:** score the ball goals
Stay loosely spread for **Deafening Screech**. At 75% and 45% HP, Crawth casts
**Ruinous Winds** which can only be stopped by grabbing 3 balls and scoring a
goal. Each goal adds a permanent field hazard for the rest of the fight.

| Ability | What it does | Do | Archetype | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Savage Peck | Heavy tank hit + DoT (NOT a bleed in Midnight). | Active mitigation every cast. | tank-buster | 🔵 | tank |
| Overpowering Gust | Frontal at a random target that knocks players away. | Sidestep out of the cone. | frontal-cone | 🟠 | all |
| Deafening Screech | Interrupts/silences casters + party-wide DoT; splash if stacked. | Stay loosely spread; group defensive / healing CD. | spread-out | 🟠 | all |
| Play Ball! (Ruinous Winds gate) | At 75%/45% Crawth channels Ruinous Winds; only interruptible by scoring 3 balls in a goal. | Grab 3 balls, throw into a goal to cancel the cast. | soak | 🔴 | all |
| Goal of the Rushing Winds → Gale Force | Scoring the air goal spawns roaming Roving Cyclones + pushback winds for the rest of the fight (grants Haste). | Recommended first goal (75%); dodge the patrolling cyclones. | ground-void-zone | 🟠 | all |
| Goal of the Searing Blaze → Firestorm | Scoring the fire goal gives Crawth +75% damage taken for 12s, then permanent **Blistering Fire** pulsing AoE + fire circles. | Recommended at 45%; pop Bloodlust/CDs into the damage amp; avoid fire pools. | ground-void-zone | 🟠 | all |

### Echo of Doragosa <!-- enc:2514 -->

**Hint:** don't greed power stacks
Unlocks after the other 3 die. Getting hit by **anything** stacks **Overwhelming
Power** (+5% Haste/stack); at 3 stacks it auto-spawns an **Arcane Rift** under you
and resets. On pull / at 100 energy she casts **Unleash Energy** spawning 2 rifts —
pull her to a corner so rifts don't clutter the floor.

| Ability | What it does | Do | Archetype | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Unleash Energy | On pull / 100 energy, spawns 2 Arcane Rifts. | Be topped before pull; tank her in a corner toward the entrance. | raid-damage | 🔴 | all |
| Overwhelming Power → Arcane Rift | Every hit stacks the buff; at 3 stacks it drops an Arcane Rift under you and resets. | Keep a couple stacks for Haste, but don't greed to 3 in a bad spot; place rifts well. | ground-void-zone | 🟠 | all |
| Arcane Rift (Uncontrolled Energy) | Rifts periodically fire **Uncontrolled Energy** orbs across the floor. | Dodge the orbs; keep rifts in tidy positions. | ground-void-zone | 🟠 | all |
| Energy Bomb | Lands on a random player. | Personal defensive; loosely spread to avoid splashing allies. | spread-out | 🟠 | all |
| Arcane Missiles | Random-target nuke; can combo with Energy Bomb. | Defensive if it overlaps Energy Bomb; healer spot-heals. | spread-out | 🟠 | all |
| Power Vacuum | Pulls all players to the boss + AoE. | Run out / use movement; tank positions her so the pull doesn't land players on a rift. | knockback | 🟠 | all |

## DPS notes (player is DPS)
- **Interrupt budget is heavy here.** Priority kicks: Ancient Branch *Healing
  Touch* (heals boss — never let it through), Corrupted Manafiend *Surge*,
  Unruly Textbook *Monotonous Lecture*, Spectral Invoker *Arcane Bolt* / *Mystic
  Brand*, Alpha Eagle *Call of the Flock*.
- **Soaks are your job too.** On Vexamus spread and soak Arcane Orbs (mind
  non-dispellable Oversurge); on Crawth help grab Play Ball! balls to stop
  Ruinous Winds.
- **Bring an enrage dispel/Soothe** for Aggravated Skitterfly's Agitation if your
  spec has one.
- **Echo of Doragosa:** ride a couple Overwhelming Power stacks for free Haste,
  but don't greed to a 3rd stack and vomit a rift onto the floor.
- Cleave windows: cluster Hungry Lashers during Germinate; keep ranged/leap mobs
  (Arcane Ravager, Skitterfly) near the pack so they can be cleaved.
