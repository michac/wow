# Windrunner Spire — Midnight S1 M+ dungeon guide

Four bosses (journal-instance **1299**): **Emberdawn** (2655),
**Derelict Duo** (2656), **Commander Kroluk** (2657),
**The Restless Heart** (2658). Boss names match the Blizzard journal
exactly. Hub-and-spoke layout: you return to the entrance via a **Soul
Conduit** after each of the first two bosses and pick the next wing.

Consequence-tier key: 🔴 wipe / 🟠 your death / 🔵 your job / ⚪ flavor.

## Route

The instance is a hub with branching wings; you clear a shared starting
pack, then choose wings, teleporting back to the hub between bosses.

1. **Starting trash (hub).** Clear the spectral guardians at the
   entrance: Dutiful Groundskeeper, Restless Steward, Spellguard Magus.
2. **Emberdawn wing (recommended first).** Head north. Mixed
   spectral + beast pack (Dragonhawks, Spindlewebs, Apex Lynx,
   Scouting Trapper, Bloated Lasher) → **Emberdawn**.
3. **Back to hub** via Soul Conduit, then **Derelict Duo wing (south).**
   Undead pack (Windrunner Soldier, Ardent Cutthroat, Fervent
   Apothecary, Zealous Reaver, Devoted Woebringer, Flesh Behemoth) →
   **Derelict Duo**.
4. **Back to hub** via Soul Conduit. A new path opens (exact location
   depends on kill order, same mobs either way) → **Commander Kroluk
   wing**. Warparty pack (Swiftshot Archer, Phalanx Breaker, Haunting
   Grunt, Spectral Axethrower, Loyal Worg, Lingering Marauder,
   Phantasmal Mystic) → **Commander Kroluk**.
5. **The Restless Heart** — final boss (no notable distinct trash
   called out by either guide before it).

### The Restless Heart <!-- enc:2658 -->

The whole fight runs on the **Turbulent Arrows** spawned by **Arrow
Rain** — touching one knocks you up, drops a puddle, and clears your
**Squall Leap** stacks. You use them to cleanse and to dodge.

| Ability | What it does | Do | Archetype | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Arrow Rain** | AoE circles; spawns **Turbulent Arrows** on the ground | Dodge the circles; the arrows are your tool for the rest of the fight | ground-void-zone | 🟠 |
| **Squall Leap** | Stacking debuff applied to the group on reposition | Clear stacks by touching a Turbulent Arrow (knocks you up); healer tracks stack count | dispel | 🔵 |
| **Turbulent Arrows** | Ground arrows; knock you airborne when touched, remove Squall Leap | Use them to cleanse and to body-block knockbacks | knockback | 🔵 |
| **Bullseye Windblast** | Cast creating an expanding ring of **Billowing Wind** | Pre-position on an arrow and use it to avoid the wave; ping which arrow you'll take so nobody doubles up | knockback | 🟠 |
| **Bolt Gale** | Continuous frontal volley channeled at a random player; heavy damage | If targeted, stand still + use a defensive (or combat-drop) to break the channel; healer spot-heals | frontal-cone | 🟠 |
| **Tempest Slash** | Tank hit with a large knockback; briefly raises your Physical damage taken | Tank active mitigation; put your back to an arrow to cancel the knockback (also clears Squall Leap) | tank-buster | 🔵 |
| **Gust Shot** | Targets a player; cleaves allies; destroys any **Storming Soulfont** it strikes | Spread out; overlap existing puddles to clear them from the arena | spread-out | 🔵 |

## DPS notes (you are DPS)

- **Bring interrupts.** This dungeon is interrupt-heavy: Spirit Bolt,
  Fire Spit, Fungal Bolt, Poison Blades, Shadow Bolt (Woebringer + Kalis),
  Chain Lightning, Interrupting Screech, Debilitating Shriek. Set an
  interrupt rotation.
- **Devoted Woebringer** is the priority kill — burst it during Pulsing
  Shriek to break the shield and stop the group-wide damage.
- **Kroluk warparty:** swap to the adds at 66%/33% (Shield Wall makes
  boss damage near-useless) and interrupt the Phantasmal Mystic's Chain
  Lightning.
- **Derelict Duo:** balance your cleave so neither boss outlives the
  other (Broken Bond enrage).
- **Dispels:** Soul Torment (Magic), Poison Spray (Poison), Curse of
  Darkness (Curse), Bolstering Flames / Ephemeral Bloodlust (purge/
  soothe). Coordinate utility.
