# M+ Rating & Seasonal Rewards — Midnight Season 1

New to M+? Your **Mythic+ Rating** (often called "IO" after the addon
raider.io) is the single number that tracks your dungeon progress. Almost
every reward below is gated on that number. This file explains how it's
earned and what each threshold unlocks. For how keys themselves work
(first key, upgrades, the resilient floor) see `keystones.md`; for loot
mechanics see `loot.md` and `../great-vault.md`.

## Mythic+ Rating (the score)

**Per-dungeon score.** Each completed run gives a base score plus a bonus
per keystone level and per affix. Completing a dungeon at all is worth a
base of **37.5**, and each keystone level adds **7.5** on top (so higher
keys = higher score). **Timing** the dungeon (beating the dungeon's
timer) gives the full score and upgrades your key; **over-time / depleting**
(finishing after the timer) still completes and scores the run, but you get
no time bonus and **no key upgrade** — so a timed run is worth meaningfully
more than the same level over-time. Your rating only goes **up**: a worse
run never lowers your score, so there is no penalty for trying a key and
finishing it slow.

**Single score per dungeon (the big Midnight change — VERIFIED).** In older
seasons you kept two separate best runs per dungeon — one on a Fortified
week, one on a Tyrannical week — combined roughly 1.5x / 0.5x. **Midnight
Season 1 effectively ends that at the level that matters.** From **+10 and
up, Fortified AND Tyrannical are both active on every run, every reset** —
there is no Fortified week vs Tyrannical week in the push bracket. So at the
levels you care about for rating, each dungeon contributes a **single best
timed run**, not two affix-split scores. (At +7–9 you still get only one of
Fortified/Tyrannical, but those levels are below where serious rating is
built.) Corroborated by Conquest Capped, accountshark, and Icy Veins.

**Total rating** is the **sum of your best score in each of the 8 dungeons**
in the Season 1 pool (Magisters' Terrace, Maisara Caverns, Nexus-Point
Xenas, Windrunner Spire, Algeth'ar Academy, Seat of the Triumvirate,
Skyreach, Pit of Saron). To raise your total efficiently you push your
**lowest-scoring dungeon**, not your best one — every dungeon counts equally.

**Affix ladder context** (full table in `season-1-overview.md`):
- +2 to +4: Lindormi's Guidance (newcomer route-help affix).
- +5 to +11: one rotating Xal'atath's Bargain + Fortified or Tyrannical.
- +10 and up: Fortified **and** Tyrannical both active (single-score bracket).
- +12 and up: Xal'atath's Guile — deaths subtract ~15s from the timer, so
  deaths become a real timer cost only this high.

## Reward thresholds

### Dungeon teleport portals
Timing a specific dungeon at **+10 or higher (within the timer)** unlocks a
**permanent teleport** to that dungeon's entrance (the "Keystone Hero:
<Dungeon>" achievement, e.g. "Keystone Hero: Magisters' Terrace"). Eight
dungeons = eight teleports. These are account-convenience teleports and are
**kept permanently** — they do **not** expire when the season ends. (Standard
M+ behavior; the season-permanence of these specific 12.0 portals is
consistent across sources but not individually called out as permanent in a
single Blizzard note — *minor unverified detail for 12.0*.)

### Titles (rating-gated)
| Achievement | Rating | Title |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| Keystone Conqueror: Season One | **1,500** | "the Umbral" |
| Keystone Master: Season One | **2,000** | (see mount below) |
| Keystone Hero: Season One | **2,500** | — |
| Keystone Legend / top 0.1% | **3,000** (Legend); top 0.1% gets a special title | "the Umbral Hero" (top 0.1%) |

Note: most sources (Conquest Capped, Wowhead, Icy Veins) agree
**Master = 2,000 / Hero = 2,500 / Legend = 3,000**. One secondary guide
(accountshark) listed Master ≈ 2,500 / Hero ≈ 3,000 — the multi-source
consensus is the table above.

### Seasonal mounts
- **Calamitous Carrion** — from **Keystone Master** (reach **2,000** rating).
  This is the headline "KSM mount" and is **seasonal**: it stops being
  obtainable when Season 1 ends.
- **Convalescent Carrion** — from **Keystone Legend** (reach **3,000**
  rating). Also seasonal.
- **Gleaming Sunmote** — tied to the **Keystone Hero (2,500)** tier per
  Conquest Capped *(item type listed as a reward at this tier — unverified
  whether mount vs cosmetic for 12.0)*.

Non-rating mounts that drop from these dungeons (farmable, not score-gated):
**Lucent Hawkstrider** (Magisters' Terrace) and **Spectral Hawkstrider**
(Windrunner Spire).

### Achievements & gear tied to rating
- **KSM (Keystone Master, 2,000)** and **KSH (Keystone Hero, 2,500)** are the
  two milestone achievements most players target. KSM is the classic
  "I'm done with the season's core goal" badge and grants the seasonal mount.
- **Gear / Great Vault** is gated by **keystone level, not rating**, but the
  two move together. Vault rewards scale with the highest keys you time;
  **+10 is the vault cap at 272** myth-track in S1 (see `../great-vault.md`).
  Timing eight dungeons in a week fills the M+ vault row. End-of-run drops:
  +6 and up = hero track; +10 and up vault = myth track (full table in
  `loot.md`).

## What to chase first (new player)

1. **Time every dungeon at +2 once.** Builds a base score in all 8 and
   teaches routes (Lindormi's Guidance helps here).
2. **Get all 8 dungeons timed and climbing together** — push your lowest
   dungeon, since total rating is the sum across all 8.
3. **Hit +10 timed in each dungeon** → unlocks all 8 **permanent teleports**
   (huge quality-of-life) and starts feeding myth-track vault rewards.
4. **Reach 2,000 rating → Keystone Master** → the **Calamitous Carrion**
   seasonal mount + KSM achievement. This is the main goal for most players.
5. **Past KSM:** push toward **2,500 (Keystone Hero)** and **3,000
   (Keystone Legend, Convalescent Carrion)** if you want the harder rewards.
