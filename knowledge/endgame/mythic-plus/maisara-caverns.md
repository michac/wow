# Maisara Caverns — Midnight S1 M+ dungeon guide

A Vilebranch troll / necrotic-soul cavern. Three bosses (journal-instance
1315): **Muro'jin and Nekraxx** (2810), **Vordaza** (2811), and
**Rak'tul, Vessel of Souls** (2812). All three boss names match the
Blizzard journal exactly. Theme shifts from a beast/hunt motif (boss 1)
to soul/necrotic magic (bosses 2 & 3).

Both guides confirm Midnight Season 1 signals: Method "season 1 of
Midnight" (upd. 31 Mar 2026); Icy Veins updated for 12.0.7 (15 Jun 2026)
and 12.0.5 (19 Apr 2026).

## Route

PUG-friendly MDT route (Method): <https://wago.io/enn7CXdfX>

1. **Prison entry / Witherbark Prisoners.** Free **8 of 12 Witherbark
   Prisoners** (only out of combat) to open the way — clear trash near
   them first. Mobs are split left/right; both paths have the same mob
   types in different pack comps.
2. **Pick a side for the buff.** Left = Ritual Concoction Buff (weak).
   Right = Hearty Vilebranch Stew (stronger — Method routes right). Both
   sides offer Freezing Traps (pacify+slow you, but grant a 5s stun
   button — good vs Lieutenant mobs).
3. **Muro'jin and Nekraxx** (boss 1) in the first arena.
4. **Mini-boss altar after boss 1.** A mini boss sits near an altar; 4
   Ritual Incense on the walls each spawn a friendly Vengeful Shade for
   60s (low value, skippable).
5. **Soul room / Vordaza approach.** Room with **Lost Souls** pathing
   away from an altar — they can't be attacked; on contact they explode
   (shadow damage + root via Cries of the Fallen). Avoid them. Exit is
   sealed: kill the **Rokh'zal** mini boss to progress.
6. **Vordaza** (boss 2).
7. **Bridge to the final boss.** Filled with previously-seen mobs plus
   the **Zil'jan** mini boss at the far end — he sends shadow orbs
   bouncing down the bridge (heavy damage + knockback) until killed.
   Cross while dodging orbs.
8. **Rak'tul, Vessel of Souls** (boss 3).

**Hint:** trap the swoop charge

casts **Revive Pet** if Nekraxx dies first; Nekraxx gains **Bestial
Wrath** stacks if Muro'jin dies first.

## DPS notes (player is DPS)

- **Boss 1:** Even your damage between the two — they must die close
  together. Take Carrion Swoop into a Freezing Trap; otherwise stay out
  of the trap field and out of Barrage cones.
- **Boss 2:** Don't waste DPS on Unstable Phantoms (99% DR) — herd them
  into collisions instead. **Bank cooldowns for Necrotic Convergence** to
  break Deathshroud fast; that's the real DPS check.
- **Boss 3:** Pre-position for Crush Souls so totems clump, then cleave
  them down. In the Soulrending Roar gauntlet, **interrupt the Malignant
  Souls** — Spectral Residue stacks speed the whole kill.
- **Trash interrupt priority:** Ritual Hexxer **Hex**, Reanimated Warrior
  **Reanimation** (self-rez — high priority), Tormented Shade **Spirit
  Rend**, Keen Headhunter **Hooked Snare**, Gloomwing Bat **Piercing
  Screech**.
