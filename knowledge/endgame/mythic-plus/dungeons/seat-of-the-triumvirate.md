# Seat of the Triumvirate — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Legion (Argus / Eredath). Midnight Season 1 M+ pool. Boss roster carried over from Legion; M+ tuning is current-season. Boss-by-boss detail is from Tier-3 Midnight guides (Method, Icy Veins) — solid but not Tier-1 verified, hence medium confidence.

## Quick facts
- 4 bosses · void-corrupted Argus stronghold · ~34 min timer.
- L'ura (final) is the wipe boss: a hard healer HPS check that ramps with stacking Anguish.

## Route / flow
- MDT route (Method): https://wago.io/9uOEdPCaw
- Trash is interrupt-heavy and has healers + add-summoners — kill/CC priority adds before bosses.
- Keep Saprish stacked with its two pets for cleave (shared health pool).

## Bosses
- **Zuraal the Ascended** — **Oozing Slam** spawns 2 Coalesced Void / oozlings: CC one (trap/paralysis), kill them before they reach the boss — if they connect (**Crashing Void**) it's a likely wipe. **Decimate**: leap to a player → puddle; dodge / drop it near the edge. **Null Palm**: dodge the frontal.
- **Saprish** — Clear all **Void Bomb** circles (cleave with **Overload**) before **Umbral Nova**; leftover bombs = heavy raid damage. **Dread Screech** (from pet): interrupt. Keep boss + pets stacked.
- **Viceroy Nezhar** — Burst all 5 **Umbral Tentacles** (channel Mind Flay) BEFORE **Collapsing Void** or you eat extra damage. During **Collapsing Void**, stand UNDER the boss (center is the safe zone). **Mind Blast** (tank target): interrupt on rotation.
- **L'ura** — Healer HPS check. **Dirge of Despair** spawns pulsing Notes (sustained group damage); break/clear Notes via **Discordant Beam** targets. **Grim Chorus** moves Notes + applies stacking **Anguish** (damage ramps — kill before stacks pile up). **Disintegrate**: dodge rotating beams. **Abyssal Lance**: tank defensive at 3 stacks.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Dread Screech** (Shadewing) — high-priority kick.
- **Summon Voidcaller** (Dark Conjuror) — interrupt every cast or adds pile up.
- **Abyssal Enhancement** (Dire Voidbender) — interrupt.
- **Shadowmend** (Ruthless Riftstalker) — interrupt the heal.

## Role notes
- Tank: hold oozes/adds off bosses; use a CD on Zuraal's Void Slash tank combo and L'ura's Abyssal Lance at 3 stacks.
- Healer: L'ura is the real test — pre-ramp before Anguish stacks; group HPS check.
- DPS: spread interrupts (don't double-kick); burst Nezhar's tentacles and clear Void Bombs on time.

## Affix notes
- Nothing dungeon-specific confirmed for 12.0. (pre-Midnight — unverified for 12.0; the heavy add/interrupt density means Bursting/Bolstering-type affixes are the obvious caution, but no S1-specific interaction was verified.)
