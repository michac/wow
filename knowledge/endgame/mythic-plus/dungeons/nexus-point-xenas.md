# Nexus-Point Xenas — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Midnight (new). Midnight Season 1 M+ pool. Confidence medium — three Midnight 12.0.7 sources agree on bosses/wings, but exact spell names and skip details are lightly corroborated and may shift between PTR and live.

## Quick facts
- 3 bosses · ~30:00 M+ timer · Voidstorm zone.
- Three wings: **Ethereal (W)** and **Void (E)** are open; clear both bosses to open the **Light wing (N)** for Lothraxion.

## Route / flow
- Pick a wing (Ethereal or Void), clear it, then the other — Light wing gates behind both.
- Kill/destroy **Mana Batteries / active batteries** dropped by Flux Engineers ASAP; they pulse damage.
- **Grand Nullifiers** and **Reformed Voidlings** spawn dangerous adds on death — control the death timing, don't cleave them blindly into a busy pull.
- Keystone.guru / MDT imports exist for PUG-safe routing; no major free skips confirmed.

## Bosses
- **Chief Corewright Kasreth (Ethereal)** — **Interrupt Arcane Zap.** **Reflux Charge:** if you get it, run into a **Leyline Array beam intersection** to destroy the array (don't just dodge — charged players clear the room). Otherwise **dodge the active beams** + Flux Collapse puddles. **Corespark Detonation** knockback → then **Sparkburn** absorb on whole party (healer pre-ramp). Boss hits via instant arcane casts, not melee.
- **Corewarden Nysarra (Void)** — **Burst window:** stand in the light frontal after **Lightscar Flare** → boss takes **300% more damage**. **Null Vanguard:** focus the **Dreadflail / adds** and interrupt **Nullify** casts — kill adds before the frontal ends or she eats them. Spread for **Eclipsing Step** DoT (don't double-stack the hits). Tank: **Umbral Lash → Void Gash** debuff (defensive / consider swap).
- **Lothraxion (Light, final)** — **Divine Guile:** he hides among **Fractured Images**; interrupt **only the real one** (the copy **with light horns / correct horns on head**). **Interrupting a wrong image = Core Exposure** (raid-wide holy blast + 20% holy taken for 1 min) — the hardest check in the dungeon. **Brilliant Dispersion** targets 3 players (spread). Tank: drop **Searing Rend** puddles away from group; they persist.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Nexus Adept — Umbra Bolt** (interrupt).
- **Lightwrought — Holy Bolt** (interrupt).
- **Circuit Seer — Arcing Mana**: immune to CC and often un-interruptible → **pop defensives** through the channel.
- **Radiant Swarm — Fixate**: CC it / kite.

## Role notes
- Tank: face boss away; pre-place Searing Rend (Lothraxion) and use defensives for Umbral Lash. Group near Leyline Arrays for melee uptime on Kasreth.
- Healer: pre-ramp for **Sparkburn** absorb (Kasreth) and **Brilliant Dispersion** (Lothraxion); biggest spike is Core Exposure if an image is botched.
- DPS: hold cooldowns for the **Lightscar Flare 300%** window (Nysarra); assign one reliable interrupter for Lothraxion's real image.

## Affix notes
- Nothing dungeon-specific confirmed for S1 affixes. (General: don't over-pull when Nullifiers/Voidlings will death-spawn adds.)
