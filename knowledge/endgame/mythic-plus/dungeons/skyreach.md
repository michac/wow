# Skyreach — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Warlords of Draenor. Midnight Season 1 M+ pool (legacy dungeon).
> Confidence medium: boss roster/order + S1 timer & affix interaction are confirmed Midnight-era; per-boss
> mechanic *cues* are corroborated across sources but most are not explicitly re-verified as 12.0-retuned —
> treat exact numbers as approximate.

## Quick facts
- 4 bosses · vertical arakkoa spire, ~28–35 min · base timer **28:00** (+2 at 22:36, +3 at 16:42).
- No hard enrages; this is a movement + interrupt check. Lots of platform edges — knockbacks kill.

## Route / flow
- Linear/vertical — little skip room. Pull tight, the danger is overlapping caster packs, not boss DPS.
- Big threats are caster trash (interrupts below), not the bosses. Don't body-pull two caster packs at once.
- Use Lindormi's Glow (low-key affix) to keep routing clean on the upward path.

## Bosses
- **Ranjit** — wind/movement fight. Dodge **Windwall / Chakram Vortex** tornadoes on sight; **Four Winds**
  splits the platform into shrinking safe ground — reposition early. Stay off edges (knockback = death). No enrage.
- **Araknath** — soft damage race. **Burst** applies stacking buffs that make later hits hit harder — healers
  **ramp ahead of each Burst, don't react**. **Intercept Energize** beams from the Lesser Construct adds
  (use defensives / body-block) to stop the boss powering up. Group defensive for **Supernova**.
- **Rukhran** — **Solar Flare** fixates a random player and chases — **CC/kite + burn it** before contact
  (heavy Sunstrike). Kill **Sunwing** adds promptly. Tank uses a CD on **Burning Claws**. LoS **Searing Quills**
  behind the central pillar. Do NOT cleave the eggs — they respawn adds.
- **High Sage Viryx** — **Cast Down** drops a Solar Zealot/add — **assign a pickup target pre-pull** or pressure
  spirals; kite it toward the entrance for distance. **Interrupt Solar Blast** (tank damage). Dodge the
  **Lens Flare** beam with movement CDs.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Solar Bolt** (Skyreach Initiate) — interrupt; raw damage.
- **Blinding Light** (Adept/Priestess caster) — interrupt; blind/disorient.
- **Repel / Gale Surge** (Wind-caller) — interrupt; knockback near edges = death.
- **Solar Orbs** when spawned — must-kill before they detonate.
- CC **Sun Talons** when they reach **Bloodcrazed** (enrage). Kite **Mark of Death** debuff targets.

## Role notes
- Tank: assign Cast Down/add pickups before the Viryx pull; CD on Burning Claws (Rukhran) and Solar Blast (Viryx).
- Healer: pre-ramp Araknath's stacking Burst; watch fixate/beam targets (Solar Flare, Searing Quills, Lens Flare).
- DPS: track your interrupt rotation on caster trash; kill priority is adds (Solar Flare, Sunwing, Cast Down) over boss.

## Affix notes
- **Xal'atath's Bargain: Pulsar** (one of the rotating +5/+7 Bargains): Void Pulsar orbs tether to players —
  **everyone soaks within ~15s** for party Mastery/Leech; ignored orbs buff enemies (~10% dmg + DR stacks).
  Soaking on a vertical, edge-heavy layout means don't chase a pulsar off a platform.
- Otherwise nothing Skyreach-specific.
