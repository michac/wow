# Algeth'ar Academy — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Dragonflight. Midnight Season 1 M+ pool (legacy dungeon, retuned).
> Confidence medium: Midnight S1 sources (Icy Veins/Method/Conquest Capped) confirm boss roster, timer, and pool, but precise 12.0.7 numeric tuning is sparse — mechanic *behavior* below is corroborated, exact HP%/damage may shift.

## Quick facts
- 4 bosses · ~29:30 timer · medium-high difficulty.
- On entry, each player picks a **Dragonflight stat buff** at the orbs: Bronze = 5% Haste (DPS), Green = 10% healing received (tank), Black 5% crit / Blue 85 mastery / Red 5% vers.
- Fixed order in practice: Overgrown Ancient → Crawth → Vexamus → Echo of Doragosa (Echo is locked until other three die).

## Route / flow
- Garden (Overgrown Ancient) section has the densest trash — big AoE pulls here, but it's also the most lethal; don't overpull awake Hungry Lashers.
- Three wings can be done flexibly, but most routes go Garden → Crawth → Vexamus; Echo last by necessity.
- Tight timer: efficient routing + clean garden pulls is the difference between timing and not.

## Bosses
- **Overgrown Ancient** — Interrupt **Healing Touch** (Ancient Branch add) or it heals up. **Germinate**: seeds spawn under players → stack/move together to drop them safely; **Burst Forth** awakens dormant Lashers (heavy group dmg — cooldown it). Kill Ancient Branch adds; rotate poison dispels on Hungry Lashers for Lasher Toxin stacks.
- **Crawth** — **Ruinous Winds** (cast at ~75% & ~45%): score 3 balls into the arena goals to stop it, or it wipes the group. Dodge **Overpowering Gust** frontals; tank uses a defensive on each **Savage Peck**. Loosely spread for **Deafening Screech** splash.
- **Vexamus** — **Arcane Orbs** (5 per set): each must be soaked *before* reaching the boss (he gains energy otherwise) — spread soaks, don't stack (Oversurge). Dodge **Arcane Fissure** circles and **Mana Bomb** puddles. Tank points **Arcane Expulsion** away from group.
- **Echo of Doragosa** — Top everyone before pull (**Unleash Energy**). Getting hit by anything stacks **Overwhelming Power**; at 3 stacks you spawn an **Arcane Rift** — move to the edge before it forms (#1 wipe cause = bad rift placement). Dodge rifts + **Uncontrolled Energy** orbs; spread for **Energy Bomb**; **Power Vacuum** pulls group to boss, move out of the AoE.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Corrupted Manafiend** — *Surge* (interrupt).
- **Unruly Textbook** — *Monotonous Lecture* (interrupt; group damage + silence).
- **Spectral Invoker** — *Mystic Brand* (priority interrupt), *Arcane Bolt*.
- **Spellbound Scepter** — *Mystic Blast* (interrupt). *(pre-Midnight — unverified for 12.0)*
- **Alpha Eagle** — *Call of the Flock* (interrupt). *(pre-Midnight — unverified for 12.0)*

## Role notes
- Tank: defensives on Savage Peck (Crawth) and tank-soak Arcane Orbs (Vexamus); point Arcane Expulsion out.
- Healer: pre-top for Burst Forth, Deafening Screech, Echo pull; dispel Mana Bombs / Lasher Toxin.
- DPS: assigned orb soaks on Vexamus, ball-scoring on Crawth, manage your own Overwhelming Power on Echo.

## Affix notes
- Nothing dungeon-specific. Standard Midnight S1 affix ladder applies (Lindormi's Guidance at +2–4, one Xal'atath's Bargain + Fort/Tyr from +5, both Fort and Tyr at +10, Xal'atath's Guile replaces Bargains at +12 — see `../season-1-overview.md`). Guile's harsher death penalty punishes the rift/orb mistakes above — play clean.
