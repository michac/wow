# Pit of Saron — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Wrath of the Lich King. Midnight Season 1 M+ pool (one of 4 legacy dungeons).
> Confidence medium: boss roster + key trash interrupts corroborated across Method/Icy Veins/Wowhead Midnight guides; some mechanic detail (Tyrannus tunnel hazard) varies between sources.

## Quick facts
- **3 bosses** · frost/shadow gauntlet, linear · ~30–31 min timer.
- Timer pressure comes from **trash, not bosses** — trash hits harder than most S1 dungeons.
- Must liberate the **Quarry Camps** in the opening area to open the path forward.

## Route / flow
- Quarry area first: clear trash + free the camps (gates the rest of the run).
- Order: **Garfrost → Ick & Krick → ice cave → Tyrannus**. Linear; few true skips.
- **Dreadpulse Lich packs are the most dangerous pulls** — pull isolated, don't chain into other packs.
- Glacieth mini-boss (Cryoburst / Cryopatch) guards the final approach to Tyrannus — dodge the frost patches.

## Bosses
- **Forgemaster Garfrost** — *Throw Saronite*: don't overlap the ground circles / spread them out. *Orebreaker*: tank defensive; break the spawned **Ore Chunk** to deny the stun. *Glacial Overload*: line-of-sight it behind a remaining Ore Chunk, then dispel the resulting Magic debuff fast.
- **Ick & Krick** — *Death Bolt*: **interrupt** Krick's random-target cast. *Shade Shift*: **interrupt** Shadowbind from spawned Shades and cleave them down. *Get 'em, Ick!*: if fixated, **kite** for the ~7s window; everyone else cleaves the boss.
- **Scourgelord Tyrannus** — *Army of the Dead*: kill **Rotlings** fast (disease stacks the tank), then **swap to Plaguespreaders and interrupt Plague Bolt**. *Rime Blast*: cleave the **Bone Piles** (green pillars) to weaken adds. *Scourgelord's Brand*: tank uses a **defensive** for the knockback + follow-up leap.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Icy Blast** (Dreadpulse Lich) — interrupt at all costs; heavy tank-buster.
- **Dread Pulse / Torrent of Misery** (Dreadpulse Lich, ~50% HP) — pulsing party-wide AoE; pre-plan defensives/externals.
- **Netherburst** (Arcanist Cadaver) — interrupt.
- **Icebolt** (Rimebone Coldwraith) — interrupt.
- **Shadow Bolt** (Gloombound Shadebringer) — interrupt.
- **Dispel:** Curse of Torment (Quarry Tormentor), Permeating Cold (Rimebone Coldwraith).

## Role notes
- **Tank:** position Ore Chunks away from where the group fights; defensives for Orebreaker, Brand, and Icy Blast; watch disease/Sunder stacks.
- **Healer:** pre-ramp for Dread Pulse / Torrent of Misery pulse phases; dispel Curse of Torment and Permeating Cold quickly.
- **DPS:** rotate interrupts on the priority casts above; cleave Bone Piles and Shades on demand; spread loosely for Saronite circles.

## Affix notes
- Nothing dungeon-specific confirmed for 12.0.7. Bursting/Afflicted-type affixes are extra punishing during Dread Pulse pulse windows — save dispels/defensives. (general — unverified for this exact dungeon)
