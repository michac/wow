# Maisara Caverns — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Midnight (new). Midnight Season 1 M+ pool.
> Note: confidence **medium** — boss kits corroborated across Wowhead/Icy Veins/Method (all Tier 3), but spell IDs and exact tuning not yet pulled from Tier 1 (wago/patch notes).

## Quick facts
- 3 bosses + 2 mini-bosses (Rokh'zal, Zil'jan). Vilebranch/Witherbark troll + necromancy theme.
- **Captive pre-req:** free **8 of 12 Witherbark Prisoners** (out of combat) before pulling boss 1 — splits across left/right paths.
- Most **interrupt-heavy** dungeon in the S1 pool; widely rated the hardest key at +10 and up. Dense trash punishes loose pulls.

## Route / flow
- Right side → grab **Hearty Vilebranch Stew** buff → free prisoners → Muro'jin & Nekraxx.
- Rokh'zal (Vordaza wing) → Vordaza → bridge (Zil'jan) → Rak'tul.
- Hit trash % before the final bridge; comfortable margin if you don't over-skip prisoners.

## Bosses
- **Muro'jin & Nekraxx** (duo) — **Kill together (swap):** Muro'jin revives Nekraxx; Nekraxx empowers/stacks if Muro'jin dies first. Bring both low, finish within ~1-2s. *Carrion Swoop:* if targeted, enter a Freezing Trap to negate dmg + stun Nekraxx. *Infected Pinions* (disease) — dispel to cut group damage; defensive if Barrage-targeted.
- **Vordaza** — *Wrest/Unstable Phantoms (kite):* kite spawned phantoms into each other to collide and explode (avoid stacking Lingering Dread past 2). *Unmake:* dodge frontal cone. *Necrotic Convergence:* burst to break her shield; dodge Coalesced Death orbs — save CDs for this phase.
- **Rak'tul, Vessel of Souls** — *Crush Souls:* kill the **Soulbind Totem** fast (cleaves 3 players); stack for cleave. *Soulrending Roar:* interrupt/CC the **Malignant Soul** add. *Volatile Essence:* sidestep ground puddles; defensives during Deathgorged Vessel.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Hex** — Ritual Hexxer (top priority; sheeps a player)
- **Spirit Rend** — Tormented Shade
- **Shadowfrost Blast** — Hollow Soulrender
- **Reanimation** — Reanimated Warrior (rezzes dead mobs)
- **Piercing Screech** — Gloomwing Bat
- **Hooked Snare** — Keen Headhunter
- Mini-bosses: **Rokh'zal** *Ritual Sacrifice* (break the cast or the pulled player dies on the altar); **Zil'jan** *Ritual Drums* (interrupt; group defensives if it goes off).

## Role notes
- **Tank:** stack bosses for cleave, keep active mitigation up; pull tight but don't grief interrupt rotations.
- **Healer:** heavy rot/disease load — pre-ramp for Lingering Dread (Vordaza) and Necrotic Convergence; dispel Infected Pinions on boss 1.
- **DPS:** interrupts win this key — assign Hex/Shadowfrost/Spirit Rend; balance damage on the duo boss so neither dies early.

## Affix notes
- Nothing dungeon-specific confirmed for 12.0.7. General: the interrupt density makes any "extra-cast" or enrage-style affix punishing — tighten kick assignments.
