# Windrunner Spire — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: Midnight (new). Midnight Season 1 M+ pool. Confidence medium: 3 Midnight sources agree on bosses + kill mechanics, but secondary spell names / exact route order vary between guides — verify against in-game/MDT on key day.

## Quick facts
- 4 bosses, linear. Windrunner-family ruins in Eversong Woods; fire / shadow / wind themes.
- Boss order: Emberdawn → Derelict Duo → Commander Kroluk → The Restless Heart.
- Nothing timed-gauntlet; standard trash-and-boss pacing. PUG route available in MDT.

## Route / flow
- Linear progression through 4 boss areas; lean on MDT's PUG route for skips.
- Dangerous packs: **Devoted Woebringers** (interrupt their absorb/shriek) and casters that throw Shadow Bolt / Chain Lightning — see trash list.
- Emberdawn arena fills with persistent fire; rotate the group one direction (counter-clockwise) and keep new fire stacked away from the safe zone.

## Bosses
- **Emberdawn** (fire/wind hawk) — **Flaming Updraft**: debuffs ~2 players → walk to the arena's outer edge/corner before it bursts (it persists as fire + spawns twisters during **Burning Gale**). **Searing Beak**: tank-buster, pop a defensive covering hit + DoT. Interrupt **Fire Breath** frontals.
- **Derelict Duo (Kalis & Latch)** — do **not** share HP; balance damage so they die together. Interrupt **Shadow Bolt** on Kalis (rotation). **Curse of Darkness** → dispel (Curse removal). **Heaving Yank** target runs behind Latch to kill the group-damage cast. Spread for **Splattering Spew**.
- **Commander Kroluk** — **Rallying Bellow** at 66% and 33% spawns adds + huge group damage and shields the boss (~99% DR) until adds die; kill adds fast and interrupt their **Chain Lightning**. **Reckless Leap**: leaps to furthest player — bait it (1 person, then tank) with a defensive. **Intimidating Shout**: stack near an ally to avoid fear.
- **The Restless Heart** (wind/arrow) — **Squall Leap**: stacking DoT that never falls off; clear it by standing on **Turbulent Arrows** puddles (knocks you airborne, soak/overlap). Dodge **Bullseye Windblast** circle and **Arrow Rain** markers. Spread for **Gust Shot**. **Bolt Gale**: stand still + defensive.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Devoted Woebringer** — **Pulsing Shriek** (absorb shield) + **Shadow Bolt**.
- **Restless Steward** — **Spirit Bolt**.
- **Phantasmal Mystic** — **Chain Lightning**.
- **Bloated Lasher** — **Fungal Bolt** (+ **Spore Dispersal** group damage).
- Add-phase Kroluk caster adds — **Chain Lightning**.

## Role notes
- Tank: bait Kroluk's **Reckless Leap**, pre-mitigate **Searing Beak** / tank-busters, manage Emberdawn rotation positioning.
- Healer: ramp for **Burning Gale** and **Rallying Bellow** add-phase group damage; watch **Squall Leap** DoT stacks on Restless Heart.
- DPS: tight interrupt rotation (Shadow Bolt / Chain Lightning), keep Derelict Duo HP even, burn Kroluk's adds before re-engaging the boss.

## Affix notes
- Nothing dungeon-specific confirmed for S1. Heavy add/group-damage windows (Rallying Bellow, Burning Gale) compound with bursting/raging-style affixes — standard play. (unverified for 12.0 specifics)
