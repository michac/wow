# Magisters' Terrace — M+ Cheat Sheet (Midnight S1)
> Origin: TBC. Midnight Season 1 M+ pool. The TBC boss lineup (Selin/Vexallus/Delrissa/Kael'thas) is GONE — Midnight uses a brand-new roster of void-touched experiments. Mechanics here are corroborated across two Midnight-specific tier-3 guides but not a tier-1 source; confidence medium.

## Quick facts
- 4 bosses · multi-floor, ~25-30 min for a clean +12 · timer ~33-34 min (generous).
- Grab the **Arcane Tome** in the library = +5% haste raid-wide, 30 min, persists through death. Every run, no exceptions.
- Library has a gauntlet: get too close to Librarian NPCs and you get teleported back to the library entrance.

## Route / flow
- Roughly linear: courtyard (boss 1) → interior → library gauntlet (mid-run) → tower → final arena.
- Grab the Arcane Tome in the library before pushing deeper.
- Watch Ethereal patrols overlapping the main hallway — pull late or you chain two packs.

## Bosses
- **Arcanotron Custos** — *Ethereal Shackles*: magic debuff on 2 players, **dispel immediately** (heavy tick, don't let it tick twice). *Arcane Expulsion*: AoE + knockback, drops a persistent puddle — **dodge / drop puddles at room edges**, tank uses terrain to stop knockback. *Refueling Protocol* (at 0 energy): boss takes +20% dmg, Energy Orbs spawn — **loosely spread and rotate soaks** (stacking Unstable Energy has a per-player cap; don't let one person eat all). Tank: defensive for *Repulsing Slam*.
- **Seranel Sunlash** — *Wave of Silence*: long-cast AoE — **everyone step into the Suppression Zone just before it lands** or eat an 8s pacify. *Runic Mark*: 2 players marked, **leave a gap between marked players** (overlap combos via Null Reaction) and clear it in the Suppression Zone. *Hastening Ward*: boss self-buff — **purge/dispel it**, else tank pops a major CD.
- **Gemellus** — *Triplicate*: splits into clones sharing one HP pool (3 at pull, 5 at 50%) — **cleave all of them; it's one shared health bar, not separate adds**. *Neural Link*: arrow under your feet points to your linked clone — **run to and touch the correct clone** to drop your debuff + the boss shield (wrong clone = shield stays). *Astral Grasp*: pull-in + moving damage aura — **run out of the pull / use a movement CD**. Drop Cosmic Sting puddles out of the way.
- **Degentrius** (final) — *Unstable Energy Ball / pizza slices*: party **spreads into slice positions and catches the bouncing ball**; a missed catch stacks Void Destruction on the whole party — repeated misses = wipe. This IS the fight. *Void Torrent*: beam cuts the arena in half — **keep players on both sides** to catch Unstable Void Essence soaks. *Hulking Fragment*: tank hit, **back away from melee, get dispelled, drop the puddle clear of the group**. *Devouring Entropy*: random long/short debuffs — **defensive if yours is long**; healer CD here.

## Trash — must-interrupt / must-CC
- **Polymorph** (Arcane Magister) — interrupt priority.
- **Pyroblast** (Blazing Pyromancer) — interrupt every cast.
- **Terror Wave** (Void Infuser) — interrupt every cast.
- **Shadow Bolt** (Dreaded Voidwalker) — spare kicks.
- Magister Apprentices stack Arcane Bolt — rotate kicks; Void-Tainted Magisters apply a healing-reduction aura, kill fast.

## Role notes
- Tank: pre-mitigate Repulsing Slam (b1) and Hulking Fragment (b4); place Hulking Fragment/Ichor puddles OUT of DPS lanes; sit between Gemellus clones for cleave.
- Healer: dispel Ethereal Shackles instantly; spot-heal Runic Mark / Devouring Entropy; save a major CD for the Degentrius Devouring Entropy window.
- DPS: rotate Energy Orb soaks (b1), hit all 3 clones (b3); Degentrius positioning is the whole fight — one missed ball catch is a near-wipe.

## Affix notes
- Bursting: Energy Orb soaks (b1) compound debuff stacking — be deliberate. *(unverified for 12.0)*
- Sanguine / Volcanic: tighten the Degentrius pizza-slice positioning. *(unverified for 12.0)*
- Otherwise nothing dungeon-specific beyond standard kick rotations on dense trash.
