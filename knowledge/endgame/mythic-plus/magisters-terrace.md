# Magisters' Terrace — Midnight Season 1 Mythic+

Journal-instance **1300**. Four bosses (journal-confirmed):
Arcanotron Custos (2659) → Seranel Sunlash (2661) → Gemellus (2660) → Degentrius (2662).

Both guides read as Midnight Season 1 (Method "season 1 of Midnight", last updated
23 Mar 2026; Icy Veins "Midnight Season 1", updated 15 Jun 2026 for Patch 12.0.7).
Boss names match the journal exactly. Trash detail is almost entirely Method-sourced
(Icy Veins covers only the Arcane Tome and bosses), so most trash claims are flagged
`confidence: low` below.

## Route

1. **Opening pull → Arcane Tome.** Clear the first handful of mobs (Arcane Sentry,
   Arcane Magister, Sunblade Enforcer, Lightward Healer) on the way down to the
   library. Grab the **Arcane Tome** — interacting grants a group-wide 5% haste buff
   (Arcane Empowerment) for 30 min that persists through death.
2. **Library gauntlet.** Work through the library fighting Animated Codex and the
   Blazing Pyromancer while avoiding the librarians (touching one teleports you back to
   the library entrance). Killing the Pyromancer opens the exit door and despawns the
   librarians.
3. **Courtyard.** New + familiar mobs (Spellwoven Familiar, Brightscale Wyrm). Drain
   the **Energy Crystals** scattered here for a temporary damage + healing increase.
4. **Arcanotron Custos** (boss 1).
5. **Path to Seranel.** Re-clears of earlier mob types plus the new Runed Spellbreaker.
6. **Seranel Sunlash** (boss 2).
7. **Cynosure of Twilight.** Climb the platform behind Seranel's arena and channel the
   Cynosure of Twilight; this spawns the Void wing's mobs, which you fight back toward
   the start of the dungeon.
8. **Void gauntlet.** Voidling, Hollowsoul Shredder, Dreaded Voidwalker, Shadowrift
   Voidcaller, Unstable Voidling, Void Infuser, Devouring Tyrant.
9. **Gemellus** (boss 3).
10. **Degentrius** (boss 4) atop Magisters' Terrace.

## DPS notes (player is DPS)

- **Interrupts are the throughput tax here.** Polymorph (Arcane Magister), Pyroblast
  (Pyromancer, CC-immune), Terror Wave (Void Infuser, CC-immune) all want kicks; Arcane
  Bolt / Shadow Bolt take spare interrupts.
- **Arcanotron:** save burst for **Refueling Protocol** (boss takes 20% more damage);
  soak Energy Orbs but watch your Unstable Energy stacks.
- **Seranel:** you personally clear Runic Mark by stepping into the Suppression Zone, and
  you must be inside it before Wave of Silence finishes or you eat an 8s pacify.
- **Gemellus:** cleave all clones (shared HP). Touch your Neural Link clone promptly.
- **Degentrius:** own your slice for the Unstable Void Essence bounce — a missed soak
  (Void Destruction stack) is a group-killer. Spread for Devouring Entropy and place
  Entropy Orbs visibly.
- Grab the **Arcane Tome** (group 5% haste, 30 min, persists death) and drain the
  courtyard **Energy Crystals** for the temporary damage/healing buff.
