# The Gulf of Memory — boss: Mul'tha'ul

Faceless One caster, "Lord of the Deeps". Final boss of the **Descent
of the Haranir** story variant (the delve has two variants; the other
ends in Lumenia). Rated one of the **tougher Midnight delve bosses**
(Icy Veins).

## Abilities

- **Hopeless Curse** (interruptible cast) — stacking Shadow DoT
  **curse**, −40% movement speed, −20% attack/cast speed. **Interrupt
  every cast**; the slow + fixate combo is what kills at high tiers.
  Dispellable (curse) — **Valeera on healing spec dispels it**.
- **Tear It Down** — spawns tentacles that slam players near them.
  Kite the boss away from tentacles; keep him centered in the arena.
- **Unanswered Call** — fixates a player for ~8 s; his damage is
  drastically amplified but he moves at a crawl. Just keep distance —
  it's a free damage window if you kite.

(Icy Veins describes Unanswered Call as a channel-amp, Method as a
fixate; consistent reading: slow-chase fixate with amped contact
damage — both say "stay away".)

## Solo tips (high tiers)

- Clear surrounding trash first — the fight is a kiting/positioning
  check and needs room.
- **Valeera: healing spec** for this variant so she dispels Hopeless
  Curse (players can't always interrupt every cast solo).
- Warlock-specific (Affliction): warlocks **cannot decurse** — the
  interrupt/dispel plan matters. **Felhunter (Spell Lock)** is worth
  considering over the usual solo Voidwalker here, since interrupting
  Hopeless Curse is the fight's core job; otherwise Voidwalker + Valeera
  healer dispels + Shadowfury/Mortal Coil as backup cast-stops. The
  fixate is trivialized by Burning Rush + Demonic Circle/Gateway.
