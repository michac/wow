## Timewalking (weekly bonus event, rotating)

Still an **event-only** system in Midnight — one expansion's TW dungeons are
up for a week at a time as part of the weekly bonus-event rotation, then
nothing until the next cycle (warcraft.wiki.gg, fetched 2026-06-05). Never
assume it's up — check the in-game calendar (volatile).

- Scales to character level (Chromie Time scaling, 11.x rework), so it's a
  legitimate alt-leveling dungeon pool **when active** — no inherent XP buff
  outside Turbulent Timeways' Knowledge/Mastery stacks (see
  `../_meta/next-patch.md`).
- Queue minimums by expansion: Classic 10 · TBC/Wrath 30 · Cata/MoP 35 ·
  WoD 40 · Legion 45 · BfA 50 · SL 60.
- **TT5 (June 30 → Aug 11)** = TW up every week back-to-back; the alt-leveling
  window for the rest of Season 1.
