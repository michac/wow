# Dawncrests (Midnight Season 1 upgrade currency)

Tiers: **Adventurer / Veteran / Champion / Hero / Myth**. Hero upgrades
Hero-track gear 259 (1/6) → 276 (6/6); **150 crests fully upgrades one piece**.

## ⚠ Caps were REMOVED (May 19 2026 hotfix)

The seasonal/weekly cap on **total** crest accumulation is **gone** (lifted
early — they were headed for 1,000). **Most of the internet still says
"100/tier/week" — that is dead info.** What this means:

- **Total Hero/Myth crests you can hold or earn = unlimited.**
- **Repeatable sources are now the farm** — grind them as much as you want.
- **BUT per-activity weekly caps survived** on *gated* sources: Bountiful
  Delves / gilded stashes still hand out only a fixed number of crests per
  week. Uncapped ≠ every source is infinite — only the *repeatable* ones are.

> Pre-May-19 the cap was 100/tier/week (= 5 upgrades/track/week). Kept here
> only so stale guides citing it can be recognised as out of date.

## Hero crest sources, by farmability

### Repeatable / uncapped (volume engine)
- **Mythic+** — scales with key level, unlimited. Best raw rate, but needs a
  5-group. Even low keys give Hero crests now that totals are uncapped.
- **Ritual Sites T5/T6** — **solo**, repeatable, ~**20 Hero/run** at T5. The
  solo player's volume engine. **T6 (12.0.7)** — new top difficulty, 6
  challenges, ilvl ~**274** recommended — pays **10 Heroic + 5 Myth
  Dawncrests** per run (the Myth crests are the new draw). Lower tiers give
  Hero crests too, just fewer — clear whatever tier you can and work up; you
  do not need T5 specifically. 12.0.7 also adds new ritual-site **weekly
  quests with bonus rolls** (Weeks 3 & 6).

### Weekly-gated (guaranteed chunk — do them, don't expect volume)
- **Bountiful T11 delve** — reported ~**35 Hero (+5 Myth)** crests; weekly-
  limited (the cap that survived). Needs the Bountiful Coffer.
- **Gilded stash** (T11 bountiful, no key) — **Hero** crests at Delver's
  Journey **rank 2**, upgrades to **Myth** at **rank 4** (~21/wk, ~3 stashes/wk).
- **Heroic raid bosses** — weekly lockout.

### One-time (free, grab once)
- **Cracked Keystone** — first T11 delve → quest → complete a +2 key →
  **20 Hero + 20 Myth**, cap-exempt.
- **Nullaeus first kill** (Torment Rise delve nemesis) — **30 Hero**, one-time.

### NOT a Hero source
- **Regular (keyless / non-Bountiful) delves** — the standard treasure-room
  chest tops out at **Champion** crests (Adventurer/Veteran/Champion by tier).
  No Hero from a plain run; Hero only via the Bountiful Coffer / gilded stash
  above. (Per ConquestCapped / Icy Veins chest breakdown, 2026-06.)
- **Nightmare Prey weekly** (20 Hero) — *was* cap-exempt, hotfixed to count
  (moot now that the total cap is gone, but it is not "bonus" crests).

## Vaskarn conversion (Silvermoon) — overflow only, not a farm
Trades **30 lower-tier → 10 next-tier** crests (needs the relevant gear-
milestone achievement). **3:1 is a bad rate** — ~450 Champion = 150 Hero =
one piece. Only worth it to drain a Champion/Veteran surplus you'll never
otherwise spend; never a reason to farm the lower tier deliberately.

## For Encomplete (solo, T11 easy, no T5 ritual / no M+ rating yet)
- **Volume Hero crests must come from Ritual Sites** (the solo repeatable
  source). T5 is currently a gear wall, not skill — the new Hero hands/feet +
  cresting should open it shortly; until then farm the highest ritual tier you
  can clear.
- **Delves give a weekly chunk, not volume**: do the Bountiful T11(s),
  gilded stash, and the one-time Cracked Keystone + Nullaeus.
- M+ is the highest-rate farm if/when willing to pug.
