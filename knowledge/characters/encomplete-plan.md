## Spend rules

- **Sparks**: never on tier slots (head/shoulders/chest/hands/legs —
  crafted gear can't be catalyzed). Bank new sparks for bad-luck slots.
- **Catalyst (6 charges)**: hold until a champion/hero-track piece
  lands in **legs or shoulders** (worst slots, both tier) → upgrade →
  catalyze → 2pc/4pc. Don't burn charges on slots you'll re-drop soon.
  ⚠ **2026-06-07 update (user-verified in-game): the new 263 shoulders
  (Abyssal Immolator's Fury) ARE a set piece already** (champion track,
  maxed 6/6) — tier piece #1 acquired without a charge.
  **2026-06-07 (later): Hero 259 LEGS dropped from the Trovehunter's
  trove** — a non-set tier-slot piece, so they're now the **best 2pc
  catalyst target**: catalyze the **259 hero legs** (beats the 253 chest
  / 246 helm by 6–13 ilvl) → 259 hero tier legs + 263 shoulders = 2pc.
  Then feed **Hero Dawncrests** (from ritual sites) into the legs to
  push past 259; bank the 84 Champion crests for a lesser slot. Hold
  remaining charges for the **12.0.7 750-accolade hero caches**
  (slot-targeted → more 259+ tier pieces toward 4pc). The old "legs
  214 catalyst target" line is dead — legs are solved.
- **Hero Dawncrests** (from ritual sites): upgrade hero-track pieces
  (coffer/vendor/vault). NOT onto crafted gear (no refund on recraft).
- **Myth Dawncrests** (rank-4 gilded stashes, 21/wk): recraft spark
  gear 259→285, weapon first.
- **Champion Dawncrests** (100+ banked): only into catalyst-bound
  pieces or long-keepers; hero sources supersede soon.
- **Field Accolades**: ~~hero pieces (500)~~ ~~BANK for 750 slot-targeted
  Hero caches~~ → **REVERSED AGAIN 2026-07-09 — spend on WARBAND caches for
