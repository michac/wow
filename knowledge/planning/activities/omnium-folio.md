The **Omnium Folio** is 12.0.7's new runic-power progression system, unlocked via the
questline **The Magisters' Call** → Theramoth/Umbric at Magisters' Terrace → **Seeking
Knowledge, the Omnium Folio** (receive folio, open, empower first rune; accessed from a
mini-map button). After the one-time unlock it's a **multi-week** track: each week's
*Seeking Knowledge* step unlocks/empowers more runes.

Tagged `goal:[gearing, story]` — it's real player power (`gearing`) delivered through a
weekly story step. **Do it early**: it's the one 12.0.7 addition tied directly to new
player power, so deferring it delays your power ceiling. `gate` is best-effort on the weekly
step ID — read the live log if it doesn't resolve.
