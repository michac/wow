last reset's M+/delve/raid/PvP progress unlocked. Free power, trivial time, **expires at
reset**. The activities that fill its columns (`mplus`, `delve-bountiful`) are their own
entries; this row is only the *claim*. Gate hides it once claimed / when empty.
