The Midnight main-story campaign chapters. **A gate for almost everything else** — completing
it unlocks World Quests, the renown tracks, Adventure Mode, and the **Hero-tree fourth lane +
Apex Talent slot**, and hands out catch-up gear (~ilvl 240). `cadence: one-time` per character;
`goal:story` is the terminal want, `gearing` the byproduct.

**Cross-character star case:** on a leveling/fresh alt (Uncomplete) this outranks any capped
weekly — it's the prerequisite that opens the whole endgame loop. On the geared main it's
already done (gate hides it). `scope:character`, scored per active row.
