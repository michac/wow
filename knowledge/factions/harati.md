# The Hara'ti

One of the 5 Midnight renown factions (Silvermoon Court, Amani Tribe,
The Hara'ti, The Singularity, Slayer's Rise). The Hara'ti are the Haranir
of **Harandar** — the renown faction tied to that zone's world content.

Renown caps at **20** (each rank ~2,500 rep), with Paragon rewards beyond.
No 12.0.7 "Revelations" changes to this faction were found in the patch notes
or hotfix log; content below is unchanged from 12.0.5.

## Quartermaster + location

- **Naynar**, "Renown Quartermaster for the Hara'ti."
- Northern portion of the **upper level of The Den** (Harandar).
- Currency: **Voidlight Marl** (some recipes also cost profession-specific
  Artisan Moxie, e.g. Artisan Tailor's/Alchemist's/Enchanter's Moxie).

## Renown track highlights (key rewards per rank)

- **Rank 2** — Moth-hunting access; belt gear (~ilvl 180).
- **Rank 8** — Champion waist armor (~ilvl 246).
- **Rank 14** — *Munchy* battle pet.
- **Rank 16** — *Fierce Grimlynx* ground mount.
- **Rank 19** — *Cerulean Sporeglider* flying mount.
- **Rank 20** — "Honorary Hara'ti" title + four class-specific armor ensembles.

The vendor also stocks cosmetic transmog (the Hara'ti weapon arsenal),
toys, profession recipes, and knowledge books.

## Weekly / repeatable renown sources

- World quests and special assignments in Harandar.
- World quests anywhere in Midnight with **Contract: The Hara'ti** active.
- Main + side storyline quests.
- Rare mob kills (once per rare).
- **Legends of the Haranir** world-event weekly quest.
- Bountiful Caches in Delves.
- Dungeon weekly quests in Silvermoon.
