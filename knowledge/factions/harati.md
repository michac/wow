# The Hara'ti

Renown faction of the Haranir. Quartermaster: **Naynar**, northern part
of The Den, Harandar (outside a tent near the river). Vendor purchases
cost **Voidlight Marl**.

## Shared renown mechanics

See `the-singularity.md` — warband-wide, 2,500 rep/rank, cap 20;
dungeon weekly (1,500, choice of faction) is the biggest targeted lever.

## Key rewards

- **Renown 8: Champion-track belt** (one of the 4 renown champion
  pieces; ilvl unverified — likely Champion 1/6 = 246).
