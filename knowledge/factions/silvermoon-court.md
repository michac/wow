# Silvermoon Court

One of the 5 Midnight renown factions. Quartermaster: **Caerdis
Fairdawn**, Saltheril's Haven, Eversong Woods. Vendor purchases cost
**Voidlight Marl**.

## Shared renown mechanics

See `the-singularity.md` — warband-wide, 2,500 rep/rank, cap 20;
dungeon weekly (1,500, choice of faction) is the biggest targeted lever.

## Voidlight Marl sinks (catch-up gear)

- **Quel'Thalas Equipment Chest** (item 259996) — warbound catch-up
  chest sold by **Agmera** (npc 261303), Silvermoon, next to the
  Timeways portal (~42.2, 58.6). **750 Voidlight Marl**, gives **ilvl
  246 Champion 1/6** (M+ baseline) gear. No prerequisite — a fresh 90
  can buy instantly. Warbound, so buy on main and mail to alt. Note the
  opportunity cost: Marl is also the renown quartermaster currency, so
  this competes with targeted renown-gear purchases.

## Key rewards

- **Renown 9: Champion-track helm** — ✅ Encomplete owns it (his 246
  Silvermoon Sunspire is presumably this; renown 10 as of 2026-06-03).
- **Renown 6: *Skill Issue: Tailoring* book** (10 KP, 75 Moxie) — see
  `../systems/tailoring-leveling.md`.
