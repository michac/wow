# The Singularity

One of the 5 Midnight renown factions. Quartermaster: **Void Researcher
Anomander**, lower level of Howling Ridge, the Voidstorm. Vendor
purchases cost **Voidlight Marl**.

## Shared renown mechanics (all 4 main factions)

- **Warband-wide from the start** — rep earned on any character counts.
- **2,500 rep per rank, cap renown 20.**
- Weekly sources: dungeon weekly quest (**1,500 rep, faction of your
  choice** — the biggest targeted lever), zone weekly events, WQs +
  special assignments, **bountiful delves 2×/week**, weekly zone rares;
  WQ Bonus Event weeks up to 2,500.

## Key rewards

- **Renown 7: Champion-track trinket** (one of the 4 renown champion
  pieces; ilvl/effect unverified — likely Champion 1/6 = 246).
