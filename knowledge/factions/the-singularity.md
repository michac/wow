# The Singularity

One of the 5 Midnight renown factions (Silvermoon Court, Amani Tribe,
The Hara'ti, The Singularity, Slayer's Rise). 12.0.7 "Revelations"
introduced no changes to this faction.

## Quartermaster + location

- **Void Researcher Anomander** — quartermaster, eastern Howling Ridge
  in **Voidstorm** (~52.6, 72.8).
- Spends **Voidlight Marl** (the faction's reputation currency); item
  costs range ~150–8,000.

## Renown track highlights (key rewards per rank)

- **Rank 2:** ilvl 180 gloves.
- **Rank 7:** *Crucible of Erratic Energies* — ilvl 246 Champion trinket
  (requires completing the Stormarion Assault quest).
- **Ranks 3–18:** progressive housing/decor unlocks (thrones, tables,
  barrels, banners, lanterns, crates, training dummies).
- **Rank 17:** *Ravenous Shredclaw* (ground mount).
- **Rank 19:** *Voidbound Stormray* (flying mount).
- **Research Console completion:** *Lab-Grown Stormray*.
- Renown caps at 20; past 20 yields Paragon Bags.

## Weekly renown sources

- World Quests & Special Assignments in Voidstorm.
- World Quests with **Contract: The Singularity** active.
- **Stormarion Assault** world event (weekly quest "Stand Your Ground").
- Silvermoon dungeon weekly quests.
- Bountiful Caches from Delves.
