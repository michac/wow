# Amani Tribe

One of the 5 Midnight renown factions (Silvermoon Court, Amani Tribe,
The Hara'ti, The Singularity, Slayer's Rise). Home turf is **Zul'Aman**;
the faction hub is **Amani'Zar Village**.

## Renown structure

20 ranks, 2500 reputation each (standard Midnight faction track).

## Quartermaster + location

- **Magovu**, in the main building at **Amani'Zar Village, Zul'Aman**
  (~45.8, 65.8).

## Weekly renown sources

- Zul'Aman campaign / zone quests, World Quests, and Special Assignments.
- Zul'Aman rare enemies.
- **Abundance** world event + the **Weekly: Abundance / Abundant Offerings**
  weekly quest.
- **Bountiful Caches** from Delves.
- Silvermoon dungeon weekly quests.

## Renown track highlights (key rewards per rank)

- **Rank 8:** unlocks the **Altar of Blessings** (Loa Blessings system, below).
- **Rank 12:** **Naloki** companion pet.
- **Rank 17:** **Amani Blessed Bear** mount.
- **Rank 19:** **Amani Windcaller** flying mount.
- **Rank 20:** **Crown of the Loa-Speaker** helm + **"Loa Speaker"** title.

Magovu also stocks cosmetics, decor, profession recipes, and player-power
items gated behind their respective ranks.

## Loa Blessings (Lorewalking with the Loa)

Activated at the **Altar of Blessings** (Rank 8). Combine one **major** loa
with one **minor** loa for zone-specific buffs while inside Zul'Aman.

- **Four major loas** (earned through the main storyline): **Akil'zon,
  Halazzi, Jan'alai, Nalorakk.**
- **Minor loas** (quests, fishing, farming, or purchase): Kulzi, Filo,
  Mot'amra, Wila'ma, Shadra, Dundun, Puul, Oe, Pahk.

## 12.0.7 "Revelations" additions

Three Amani-troll story beats land with **12.0.7 (live 2026-06-16):**

- **Zul'jan Campaign Chapter** — the next Midnight campaign chapter, opening
  **July 7, 2026**. Zul'jan attends a special **haranir** gathering as the
  Amani Troll representative and is drawn into an ancient mystery about the
  Amani Trolls' legacy (involves his uncle **Kinduru**). This chapter
  **leads directly into the 12.1 campaign.**
- **Jan'alai egg-hatching** — a short Zul'Aman questline helping **Loa Speaker
  Brek** welcome the Fire Loa Jan'alai's first new clutch of eggs into the
  world. Reward: the **Emberlyn** companion pet.
- **Troll Lorewalking** — story content with **Assistant Lorewalker Li Li**
  exploring loa and troll-tribe lore across the **Darkspear, Drakkari,
  Gurubashi, and Zandalari**. Lore/flavor content, not high-pressure
  progression.
