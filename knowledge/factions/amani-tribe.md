# Amani Tribe

One of the 5 Midnight renown factions. Quartermaster: **Magovu**, right
side of the large hall in Amani'zar Village, Zul'Aman. Vendor purchases
cost **Voidlight Marl**.

## Shared renown mechanics

See `the-singularity.md` — warband-wide, 2,500 rep/rank, cap 20;
dungeon weekly (1,500, choice of faction) is the biggest targeted lever.

## Key rewards

- **Renown 9: Champion-track necklace** (one of the 4 renown champion
  pieces; ilvl unverified — likely Champion 1/6 = 246; renown gear is
  typically unsocketed — check before replacing a socketed neck).
