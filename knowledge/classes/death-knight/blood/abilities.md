# Blood Death Knight — Ability Inventory (Midnight S1)

## Overview

Blood is the Death Knight tank spec: a self-healing, resource-cycling bruiser
that mitigates spike damage with **Death Strike** (a Runic-Power heal scaled to
recent damage taken) and passive physical mitigation from **Bone Shield** stacks
(built by **Marrowrend** / **Death's Caress**). It is a two-resource spec —
**Runes** (6, recharging) fuel builders; **Runic Power** (spent on Death Strike)
is generated by nearly everything. The core loop is: keep Bone Shield up, keep
**Blood Plague** (from Blood Boil) ticking, never overcap Runic Power or waste
Death Strike, and press builders to keep runes recharging.

Two hero trees in S1: **San'layn** (Vampiric Strike / Essence of the Blood Queen
buff-stacking, favored in raid) and **Deathbringer** (Reaper's Mark stacking +
Exterminate, favored in M+). See `builds.md` and `rotation.md`.
