### Apex Talents

**Apex talents** are a new feature in Midnight that extend each Spec Talent Tree. You can allocate 4 talent points to reap the full benefits of these special talents.

* The first talent point unlocks Rank 1.
  * The second and third talent points unlock and enhance Rank 2.
  * The fourth point unlocks Rank 3 of the **Apex Talent**.

As **Demonology Warlock** , you have access to [Dominion of Argus](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1276163), which is essentially a new version of [Nether Portal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267217). For 15 seconds after casting [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125850) you want to cast as many [Hand of Gul'dans](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125834) as possible.

**Rank 2** increases the duration of the demons you spawn from [Dominion of Argus](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1276163). While **Rank 3** makes each [Hand of Gul'dan](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125834) refund 1 [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756) which allows you to cast way more of them during the 15 sec window.

### Core Changes

Following you receive a list of meaningful class changes going from The War Within into Midnight to keep you updated. For a more detailed look of **all** changes, check out our **Changes This Patch** section.

* **Spec Tree**
    * [Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125836)
      * Changed to have a 15 second cooldown and also capped to 6 [Wild Imps](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104317).
    * [Summon Doomguard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125863)
      * The random Doomguards from [Pact of the Ered'ruin](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=453568) got replaced with a 2 min cooldown.
    * [Grimoire: Fel Ravager](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136725) / [Grimoire: Imp Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136726)
      * Replacement cooldown for [Grimoire: Felguard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=111898), this gives you an additional use of [Spell Lock](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=19647) / [Singe Magic](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=89808).
  * **Class Tree**
    * [Foul Mouth](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136100)
      * Great talent to apply [Curse of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=702), [Curse of Exhaustion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=334275) or [Curse of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1714) to a group of enemies at once.
    * [Frequent Traveler](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136101)
      * More [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91466) mobility!
    * [Blight of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136108) / [Blight of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136738)
      * Powerful new AoE curses to slow down multiple enemies' attack or castspeed.
    * [Infernal Beneficiary](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136104)
      * Direct [Health Funnel](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=40671) replacement.
  * **Removed**
    * [Soul Conduit](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=215941)
      * This results in fewer available [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) and less randomness in your [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) economy.
    * [Soul Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264057)
      * Reduces your [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) generation slightly.
    * [Bilescourge Bombers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267211) / [Demonic Strength](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267171)
      * Decent nerf to the on-demand AoE burst.

* [Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385) and [Soul Harvester](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123383) have pretty similar damage profiles. With [Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385) your burst damage through [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187) becomes a lot stronger and [Soul Harvester](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123383) emphasizes [Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125836) and AoE damage.
  * Currently [Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385) performs slightly better in almost all scenarios.

**[Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385)**

* Spending a [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) starts a cycle of one of 3 different [Diabolic Rituals](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428514). 
    1. [Diabolic Ritual: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=431944)
    2. [Diabolic Ritual: Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432815)
    3. [Diabolic Ritual: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432816)
    4. Starts on 1. again.

* Only 1 [Diabolic Ritual](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428514) can be up at a time and each turns into their respective **Demonic Art** after it expires which is triggered after you spend your next [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985):
    * [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524)
      * A Fel Lord jumps at your target and applies [Cloven Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434424) for 15 seconds.
    * [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794)
      * Deals damage to your target and turns your next [Shadow Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=686) into [Infernal Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434506) which generates 3 [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985).
    * [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795)
      * Summons a Pit Lord that attacks your target with [Felseeker](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434404) and turns your next [Hand of Gul'dan](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=105174) into [Ruination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434635) which summons 3 [Wild Imps](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104317) at a 0 [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756) cost.
  * Your [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187) windows become a lot more important with [Abyssal Dominion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=429581).
  * [Diabolic Oculi](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136092)
    * Passive AoE damage when consuming [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524), [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794) or [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795).
  * [Looks That Kill](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136091)
    * Additional single-target damage when consuming [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524), [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794) or [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795).
  * [Mind's Eyes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136090)
    * Every time you consume [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524), [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794) or [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795). You gain 2% intellect per consumed [Diabolic Oculi](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136092) up to 6% for 10 seconds.

**[Soul Harvester](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123383)**

* [Succulent Souls](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793) empower your [Hand of Gul'dan](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=105174) and makes it apply [Soul Anathema](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117444).
    * [Succulent Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793)
      * Every time you generate a [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756) it has a chance to give you a [Succulent Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793).
      * [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187) grants you 3 [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756) and 3 [Succulent Souls](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793).
    * [Soul Anathema](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117444)
      * [Hand of Gul'dan](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=105174) applies [Soul Anathema](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117444) to all targets hit.
      * Consuming a [Demonic Core](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102) applies [Soul Anathema](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117444) to your current target.
    * [Shared Fate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117420)
      * When you consume a [Demonic Core](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102) your target gets afflicted by [Shared Fate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117420).
      * During AoE scenarios it's important to never cast [Demonbolts](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264178) on targets that are too far away to benefit from [Shared Fate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117420) AoE damage.
    * [Sataiel's Volition](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117435)
      * Combined with [Demoniac](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125834) you now have a 15% chance per [Wild Imp](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104317) to receive [Demonic Core](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102) after casting [Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125836).
      * Make sure to be on a low amount of [Demonic Core](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102) stacks before casting [Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125836).
    * [Manifested Avarice](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136098)
      * Each [Succulent Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793) consumed has an increasing chance to unleash the [Demonic Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449614) within you, enabling it to assault enemies with [Soul Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1269049).
    * [Shared Vessel](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136097)
      * Increases your Mastery by 4%.
      * This effect is doubled while the demonic entity is aiding you in combat.
    * [Eternal Hunger](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136096)
      * Increases the duration of [Manifested Avarice](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136098) by 5 sec and increases the damage of [Soul Swipe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1269049) by 10%.

### Gameplay Altering Talents

Discover all talents that significantly alter your gameplay within the Spec and Class Talent trees. This section gives a concise overview of these talents and their applications but for a more detailed look check out the Rotation and **Deep Dive** sections below.

#### Spec Tree

* [Call Dreadstalkers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104316)
    * One of your key abilities which is enhanced by a multitude of talents.
    * [Demonic Calling](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205145) allows you to cast [Call Dreadstalkers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104316) for free and without cast time.
    * [Summon Vilefiend](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135314) spawns with your [Call Dreadstalkers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104316) which makes it even more important to use [Call Dreadstalkers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104316) on cooldown.
  * [Power Siphon](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264130)
    * Significantly boosts your influx of [Demonic Cores](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102).
    * Make sure to always have 2+ [Wild Imp](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=279910) before you use it.
  * [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187)
    * The main cooldown for **Demonology** , synergizes with the majority of spells within the spec. 
  * [Grimoire: Fel Ravager](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136725) / [Grimoire: Imp Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136726)
    * Strong 2 min cooldown that is a big portion of your damage, always use this in combination with [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187).
  * [Summon Doomguard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125863)
    * Another 2 min cooldown, this one doesn't benefit from the buffs of [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187) so you can use it more freely.
  *

#### Class Tree

* [Fel Domination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91439)
    * Used as a quick pet recovery either if your current pet dies or you got resurrected. Skips the [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) cost and significantly reduces the cast time for the summoned pet.
  * [Burning Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91460)
    * Toggleable movementspeed increase that costs health per second to use.
  * [Mortal Coil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91452)
    * Quick selfheal on a short cooldown. Further enhanced by [Improved Mortal Coil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136105).
  * [Dark Pact](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91444)
    * Provides you with a big absorbshield at the cost of your heatlh. Health cost is reduced by [Ichor of Devils](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91446). You can also change [Ichor of Devils](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91446) to [Frequent Donor](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91445) to have access to more potential [Dark Pact](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91444) uses.
  * [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91466)
    * Enables you to travel a larger distance once every 90 seconds. You are able to use it twice if [Frequent Traveler](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136101) is talented.
  * [Soulburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91469)
    * Enhances multiple utility spells, mainly used together with [Demonic Circle: Teleport](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48020), [Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=6262), [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=111771) and [Drain Life](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=234153).
  * [Pact of Gluttony](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91434)
    * Massive for survivability since it alters your [Demonic Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452930) to be usable multiple times per combat.

### Priority List

This is a general priority you aim to maintain throughout the fight.

1. Cast [Call Dreadstalkers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104316) and [Grimoire: Imp Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136726) before [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187).

  3. Cast [Summon Doomguard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125863) on cooldown.
  4. If talented cast [Power Siphon](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136731) when you have 2+ [Wild Imps](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104317) and 2 or less [Demonic Cores](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102).
  5. Cast [Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125836) on cooldown with you have 6 [Wild Imps](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104317) if talented.
  6. Cast [Hand of Gul'dan](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=105174) / [Ruination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434635) if you are about to overcap on [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756).
  7. Cast [Infernal Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=433891) if you are below 3 [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756) and [Infernal Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=433891) is up.
  8. Cast [Demonbolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264178) if you have 2+ stacks of [Demonic Core](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=267102).
  9. Cast [Hand of Gul'dan](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=105174) with 3 [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756).
  10. Cast [Shadow Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=686) to generate [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=104756).

### Minmaxing [Doom](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136729)

When [Doom](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136729) expires it deals a high amount of damage to the target and all enemies within 10 yards.

* **Single-target**
    * To ensure maximum uptime of [Doom](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136729) it is beneficial to keep at least 1 [Demonic Core](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264173) in the back pocket to avoid periods of downtime on [Doom](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136729). 
    * While doing this it's important to not overcap on [Demonic Cores](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264173) as the maximum amount of charges is 4.
  * **Multi-target**
    * When facing multiple targets, prioritize applying [Doom](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136729) to different targets for more explosions. During large pulls, tab between each [Demonbolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=264178) to cycle through targets.

### [Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125850) Mechanics

Your [Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125850) is a so called **Guardian** pet that works a bit differently compared to your primary pet called [Felguard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=30146) that is a combat pet. [Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125850) targets whatever your [Felguard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=30146) targets.

#### Guardian Pet Stat Update

[Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=125850) like many other **Guardians** does update dynamically with stat increases such as haste but sometimes does so very slowly.

For min-maxing a **Demonology** Warlock** in raiding, different racial traits can provide a tremendous benefit to your character. If this is not your top goal, picking a race that fits your style works just as well.

* [Stoneform](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=65116) ___\-- Dwarf___
    * Dispels Magic and Bleed debuffs. Has been useful in the past on some bosses with Bleeds.
  * [Rocket Jump](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=69070) ___\-- Goblin___
    * Do a small jump in the direction your character is facing.
    * While you're in the [Rocket Jump](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=69070) animation you are placed on a global cooldown until your character lands.
  * [Berserking](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=26297) ___\-- Troll___
    * Strong DPS racial but lines up poorly with [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187).
    * Reduce the duration of movement-impairing effects by 20%. This was only useful once in the recent history of progression raiding but is still worth noting.
  * [Blood Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=33702) ______\-- Orc___ ___
    * Strong racial for damage because of both [Command](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=21563) and [Blood Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=33702) which lines up with [Summon Demonic Tyrant](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=265187).
    * 20% reduced stun duration on you which can be useful in some situations.
  * [Spatial Rift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=256948) _\-- Void Elf_
    * Spawn a shadow traveling into your facing direction, and activate it again to teleport to it.
    * Maximum range of 100 yards.

### Recommendation

In general, it's safe to say that if you care about min-maxing your DPS, you should go with the highest DPS racial. That being said, the story is a bit different if it's about progression raiding. Some help out massively to speed up the progression on certain bosses. Notably, Dwarf with their [Stoneform](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=65116) helped out on **Tindral Sageswift** and **Fyrakk the Blazing** in the latest Race to World First by having easy access to dispelling yourself at crucial points of the fights mentioned.
