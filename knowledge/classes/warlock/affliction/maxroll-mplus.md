### Apex Talents

**Apex talents** are a new feature in Midnight that extend each Spec Talent Tree. You can allocate 4 talent points to reap the full benefits of these special talents.

* The first talent point unlocks Rank 1.
  * The second and third talent point unlocks and enhances Rank 2.
  * The fourth point unlocks Rank 3 of the **Apex Talent**.

As an **Afffliction Warlock** , you have access to Shadow of Nathreza, which empowers your [Haunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48181) to deal a small amount of cleave damage.

**Rank 2** increases the initial damage and increases the bonus damage haunt provides to your other abilities. While **Rank 3** has a chance to call the demonic soul within the [Haunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48181) causing a meteor to fall on your target which deals large AoE damage.

Following you receive a list of meaningful class changes going from The War Within into Midnight to keep you updated. For a more detailed look of **all** changes, check out our **Changes This Patch** section.

* **Spec Tree**
    * [Unstable Affliction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=30108)

**[Soul Harvester](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123384)**

* Generating a [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) now has a chance to grant a [Succulent Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793).
    * [Succulent Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793) empowers your next [Unstable Affliction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=30108) cast.
  * [Sataiel's Volition](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449637) makes [Haunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91552) grant you a [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=108558) proc.
    * Be careful to not overcap on [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=108558) stacks as [Sataiel's Volition](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449637) also makes your [Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=146739) tick faster which leads to more frequent procs.
  * [Necrolyte Teachings](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449620) increases [Shadow Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=686) and [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) damage and amplifies [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=108558).
  * [Shadow of Death](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449638) makes your [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052) grant 3 [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) that each contain a [Succulent Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=449793).
  * [Manifested Avarice](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1268884) causes you to occasionally unleash a demonic soul that cleaves enemies.

**[Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123382)**

* Your [Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=146739) is turned into [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465).
  * You have access to a 1-minute cooldown called [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=430014).
  * [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) can stack up and has multiple neat interactions with different talents which can increase its stack count or have other benefits.
    * Spending [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) on damaging spells increases the stacks of [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) by 1 and has a chance to gain an additional stack due to [Blackened Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117434).
    * Casting [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=430014) increases all active [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) stacks by 6 while each [Malefic Rapture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=324536) adds 1 additional stack during its duration.
    * [Mark of Peroth'arn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117441) gives [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) the chance to add a stack whenever it crits.
  * [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) has a built-in mechanic which consumes its' accumulated stacks one after another for more damage. This mechanic is called **Acute**.
    * The **Acute** state of [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) is triggered by the following: 
      * Chance whenever [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) gains a stack.
      * If [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) reaches 8 stacks.
      * If the target [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) is on reaches 20% hp or is below 20% already.
      * When [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117439) is up.
  * [Devil Fruit](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1266805) Wither's periodic effect now has a chance to proc [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=430014)

### Gameplay Altering Talents

Discover all talents that significantly alter your gameplay within the Spec and Class Talent trees. This section gives a concise overview of these talents and their applications but for a more detailed look check out the **[Rotation](https://maxroll.gg/wow/class-guides/affliction-warlock-mythic-plus-guide#rotation-header) **and **[Deep Dive](https://maxroll.gg/wow/class-guides/affliction-warlock-mythic-plus-guide#deep-dive-header)** sections below.

#### Spec Tree

* [Seed of Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=27243)
    * Allows you to spread your [Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=146739) on a large number of targets quickly. Costs 1 [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985).
  * [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052)
    * Strong AoE cooldown that does heavy damage to all targets currently DoTed and generates shards.
    * [Cull the Weak](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1259886) reduces the cooldown of [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052)
  * [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=108558)
    * Buff that reduces the channel time of your next [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198590) and increases its damage. Stacks up to 2.
  * [Haunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91552)
    * Debuffs your target to take increased damage from your abilities.
  * [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180)
    * Main cooldown for burst windows. 
    * [Nether Plating](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1280733) also grants the ability to cleave up to 5 total targets.
  * [Cunning Cruelty](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91576)
    * Boost your filler windows with passive AoE during your [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=124692)
  * [Shared Agony](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1259825)
    * Allows you to maintain agony on multiple targets.

#### Class Tree

* [Fel Domination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91439)
    * Helps you recover quickly in the event of death by allowing you to resummon a pet rapidly without cost.
  * [Burning Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91460)
    * Your main mobility spell aside from [Demonic Circle](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=124694) and [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91466), however, both of which require preplanning.
  * [Blight of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1271802)
    * Allows you to cast an empowered AoE [Curse of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1714) effect.
  * [Blight of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265799)
    * Allows you to cast an empowered AoE [Curse of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=702) effect.
  * [Soulburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91469)
    * Empowers a multitude of utility spells for the cost of 1 [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985).
    * Mainly used for [Demonic Circle: Teleport](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48020), [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91466) and [Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=6262).
  * [Shadowfury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=30283)
    * Your only AoE CC that can affect an unlimited amount of targets.
  * [Mortal Coil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=6789)
    * A single-target CC that can also be used as a defensive spell if you need to heal on demand.
    * Keep in mind that coiled and feared targets generally run **away** from you, so position yourself properly before casting it if needed.
    * [Improved Mortal Coil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265816) increases the healing and range of Mortal Coil.
  * [Dark Pact](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91444)
    * A defensive spell that grants you a big absorb shield depending on your **current** health plus a certain amount on top of it.
  * [Pact of Gluttony](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91434)
    * Combining [Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=6262) with [Soulburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91469) becomes a strong 1 minute defensive CD on long boss fights.

### Priority List

1. Keep up [Agony](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=980) on up to 5 targets.
  2. Cast [Haunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48181) on cooldown on the main target.
  3. Cast [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) on cooldown.
  4. Cast [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052) on cooldown.
  5. Cast [Seed of Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91571) if you are about to cap on [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) proc.
  6. Cast [Seed of Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91571) if you have [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=108558) procs.
  7. Cast [Seed of Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91571) whenever you have [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985).
  8. Cast [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) as your filler spell.

While [Haunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48181), [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) and [Unstable Affliction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=316099) could lower your overall dps in big pulls, it's still a damage gain on a priority target. And the way modern WoW works is that every single pull has at least one or two important trash mobs that are beneficial to kill fast to make the pull easier.

### **Burn Windows**

As an **Affliction** Warlock**, you want to cast as many [Unstable Affliction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=316099) as possible during your **Burn Windows**.

* **Burn Window** **is whenever you have [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) ready. Your main goal is to save as many [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) as you can for this cooldown and cast as many [Unstable Affliction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=316099) as possible in that window. **

With experience, you get a better understanding of how many [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) you want to spend outside of your burns, which varies on the number of targets you fight.

### Managing [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985)

The main goal of both [Soul Harvester](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123384) and [Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123387) is to spend as many shards while you have [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) active as possible. This means prior to using [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180), you want to have 4 to 5 shards and [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052) ready. This allows you to spend a large amount of [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) right after you use [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180), allowing for the highest output.

### [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91568)

With the addition of **hero talents** , the amount of procs you get over the fight is significantly increased. To the point that managing them and not overcapping is as important as keeping up your DoTs and not failing your **Burn Windows**.

Soul Harvester increases the amount of [Nightfalls](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91568) you get with [Sataiel's Volition](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117435).

The difference between [Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123387) and [Soul Harvester](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123384) is quite big to the point that you need to manage your [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) differently depending on the spec.

### [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) clipping

Clipping means manually interrupting your cast. It is not necessary to finish your [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) channel cast **unless** it is buffed by [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91568). So any time you cast [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) without it, you clip it whenever any other spell higher in priority comes in.

For example, if you are casting normal unbuffed [Drain Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388667) and you get a [Nightfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91568) proc then you instantly clip your cast and spend the proc.

### [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052)

Although the usage of this spell is pretty straightforward, there are a couple of things you need to keep in mind:

* [Dark Harvest](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257052) is a 1 minute cooldown that gets reduced every time we cast [Seed of Corruption](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91571) or [Unstable Affliction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=316099), therefore; it is crucial we are spending shards efficiently to reduce this powerful spell's cooldown as much as possible.

### Optimizing [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180)

The main feature of [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) is its increased damage per DoT on the current target. For optimal use of [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180), you want to spend as many shards as possible in quick succession to ramp up his damage.

Another important thing to know is that [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) is a **guardian** and how it updates its stats. It happens differently the moment [Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) is summoned and when it is already up.

Once you cast [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180), it snapshots your character's stats instantly but when it is already active, it may take a few seconds to update. That is why you press potion, racial and any on use stat trinket right before [Summon Darkglare](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205180) and not after.
