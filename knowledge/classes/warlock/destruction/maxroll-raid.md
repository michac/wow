### Apex Talents

**Apex talents** are a new feature in Midnight that serve as an extension of each Spec Talent Tree. You can allocate 4 talent points to reap the full benefits of these special talents.

* The first talent point unlocks Rank 1.
  * The second and third talent point unlocks and enhances Rank 2.
  * The fourth point unlocks Rank 3 of the **Apex Talent**.

As **Destruction Warlock** , you have access to **[Embers of Nihilam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=110406)** , which gives your [Incinerate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=29722) a chance to proc [Echo of Sargeras](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265884), which deals AoE Shadowflame damage to all enemies within 10 yards of your target.

**Rank 2** increases your **Crit** and **Haste** for a couple of seconds whenever [Echo of Sargeras](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265884) deals damage. While **Rank 3** enables [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136835), [Rain of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91592) and [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582) to proc [Echo of Sargeras](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265884) at 50-60% effectiveness.

### Core Changes

Following you receive a list of meaningful class changes going from The War Within into Midnight to keep you updated. For a more detailed look of **all** changes, check out our **Changes This Patch** section.

* **Spec Tree**
    * [Roaring Blaze](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91588)
      * Changed from a target debuff that increases damage from various spells to a [Conflagrate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91591) damage amplifier while letting you spread your [Immolates](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348) to nearby targets upon using [Conflagrate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91591).
    * [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582)
      * Changed from its old version [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=295422), which was usable at all times with some benefits during execute, to an execute-only spell unless the changed [Fiendish Cruelty](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=126004) procs.
    * [Improved Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91474)
      * Changed from [Rolling Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=387569).
    * [Lake of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=126493)
      * New enhancement for your [Cataclysm](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=152108).
    * [Inferno](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91489)
      * Turns your [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91502) into a 1 minute and 30 seconds cooldown!
    * [Avatar of Destruction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136833)
      * Now summoned by either your [Soul Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134221) or [Dimensional Rift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=128600).
    * [Dimensional Rift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=128600)
      * Now only a passive talent that can proc from using [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136835) or [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582).
    * [Channel Demonfire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=128599)
      * Got moved to the capstone talent category and lost its potential [Demonfire Mastery](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=456946) enhancement.
  * **Class Tree**
    * [Foul Mouth](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136100)
      * Great talent to apply [Curse of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=702), [Curse of Exhaustion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=334275) or [Curse of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1714) to a group of enemies at once.
    * [Frequent Traveler](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136101)
      * More [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91466) mobility!
    * [Blight of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136108) / [Blight of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136738)
      * Powerful new AoE curses to slow down multiple enemies' attack or castspeed.
    * [Infernal Beneficiary](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136104)
      * Direct [Health Funnel](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=40671) replacement.
  * **Removed**
    * [Soul Conduit](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=215941)
      * This results in fewer available [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) and less randomness in your [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) economy.
    * [Eradication](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=196414)
      * [Chaos Bolts](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136835) can be cast more freely now. No need to monitor any affected debuffs!
    * [Ritual of Ruin](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=387156) / [Master Ritualist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=387165)
      * Another hit to our [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) generation and also causes less frequent spawns of [Avatar of Destruction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136833) since it only works with [Soul Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134221) or [Dimensional Rift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=128600) now.

* [Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385) and [Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123382) are both playable on single-target, while [Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123382) does overall more AoE damage. [Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123382) excels in spread out cleave and mass AoE through [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) while [Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385) has a lot of passive AoE and more priority damage due to [Diabolic Rituals](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117452).

**[Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385)**

* Spending a [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) starts a cycle of one of 3 different [Diabolic Rituals](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117452). 
    1. [Diabolic Ritual: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=431944)
    2. [Diabolic Ritual: Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432815)
    3. [Diabolic Ritual: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432816)
    4. Starts on 1. again.

* The duration of an active [Diabolic Ritual](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117452) is reduced by 1 second per [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858), [Rain of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5740) or [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582) cast baseline. Your [Diabolic Ritual](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117452) is reduced by **1** additional second when you cast [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858) because of [Touch of Rancora](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117453) as well as another second if you're talented into [Infernal Machine](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=118838) and your [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91502) is active.

* Only 1 [Diabolic Ritual](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117452) can be up at a time, and each turns into their respective **Demonic Art** after it expires, which is triggered after you cast your next [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858), [Rain of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5740) or [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582):
    * [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524)
      * A **Fel Lord** jumps at your target and applies [Cloven Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434424) for 15 seconds.
    * [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794)
      * Deals damage to your target and turns your next [Incinerate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=29722) into [Infernal Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434506), which generates 2 [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985).
    * [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795)
      * Summons a **Pit Lord** that attacks your target with [Felseeker](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434404) and turns your next [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858) into [Ruination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=434635) which deals a large amount of AoE damage.
  * Additionally, whenever a **Demonic Art** is active, your next [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858), [Rain of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5740) or [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582) benefits from [Touch of Rancora](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117453).
  * Your [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91502) is further empowered through [Abyssal Dominion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117428).
  * [Diabolic Oculi](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136092)
    * Passive AoE damage when consuming [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524), [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794) or [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795).
  * [Looks That Kill](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136091)
    * Additional single-target damage when consuming [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524), [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794) or [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795).
  * [Mind's Eyes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136090)
    * Every time you consume [Demonic Art: Overlord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=428524), [Demonic Art:Mother of Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432794) or [Demonic Art: Pit Lord](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=432795). You gain 2% intellect per consumed [Diabolic Oculi](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136092) up to 6% for 10 seconds.

**[Hellcaller](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123382)**

* Your [Immolate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348) is turned into [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465).
  * [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) has no cast time or **FACING** requirement.
  * You have access to a 1 minute cooldown called [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442726).

* [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) can stack up and has multiple neat interactions with different talents which can increase its stack count or have other benefits.
    * Spending [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) on damaging spells increases the stacks of [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) by 1 and has a chance to gain an additional stack due to [Blackened Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117434) and [Bleakheart Tactics](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123310).
    * Casting [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442726) increases all active [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) stacks by 3 while each [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858), [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582) or [Rain of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91592) adds 1 additional stack during its duration.
    * [Mark of Peroth'arn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117441) gives [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) the chance to add a stack whenever it crits.
    * Whenever [Blackened Soul](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117434) deals damage you have a chance to gain [Flashpoint](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91485) stacks through [Seeds of Their Demise](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=440055) even if you are not currently talented into [Flashpoint](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91485).
  * [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) has a built-in mechanic which consumes its' accumulated stacks one after another for more damage. This mechanic is called **Corrupted**.
    * The **Corrupted** state of [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) is triggered by the following: 
      * Chance whenever [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) gains a stack.
      * If [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) reaches 8 stacks.
      * If the target [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) is on reaches 20% or is below 20% already.
      * When [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442726) is up.
  * [Through the Felvine](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136095)
    * Passive damage increase to Chaos Bolt and Rain of Fire which is doubled during [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442726).
  * [Devil Fruit](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136094)
    * [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) has a chance to grant you 8 seconds of [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442726).
  * [Alzzin's Iniquity](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136093)
    * [Malevolence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442726) grants more haste and increases [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=445465) stack size upon use by an additional 2 stacks.

### Gameplay Altering Talents

Discover all talents that significantly alter your gameplay within the Spec and Class Talent trees. This section gives a concise overview of these talents and their applications but for a more detailed look, check out the **Rotation** and **Deep Dive** sections below.

#### Spec Tree

* [Shadowburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91582)
    * Strong execute ability, usable when your enemy is below 20% health or [Fiendish Cruelty](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=126004) is up.
  * [Roaring Blaze](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=205184)
    * Got reworked in this patch, it's not a debuff anymore but rather only increases your [Conflagrate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91591) damage and spreads [Immolate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348) upon use.
  * [Soul Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134221)
    * A hard hitting spell which procs [Avatar of Destruction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136833) and generates you a Soul Shard.
  * [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1122)
    * Your main cooldown for burst windows, this is enhanced by [Crashing Chaos](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91473) and [Inferno](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91489).

#### Class Tree

* [Fel Domination](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91439)
    * Used as a quick pet recovery either if your current pet dies or you got resurrected. Skips the [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) cost and significantly reduces the cast time for the summoned pet.
  * [Burning Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91460)
    * Toggeable movementspeed increase that costs health per second to use.
  * [Mortal Coil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91452)
    * Quick selfheal on a short cooldown. Further enhanced by [Improved Mortal Coil](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136105).
  * [Dark Pact](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91444)
    * Provides you with a big absorbshield at the cost of your heatlh. Health cost is reduced by [Ichor of Devils](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91446). You can also change [Ichor of Devils](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91446) to [Frequent Donor](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91445) to have access to more potential [Dark Pact](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91444) uses.
  * [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91466)
    * Enables you to travel a larger distance once every 90 seconds. You are able to use it twice if [Frequent Traveler](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136101) is talented.
  * [Soulburn](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91469)
    * Enhances multiple utility spells, mainly used together with [Demonic Circle: Teleport](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=48020), [Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=6262), [Demonic Gateway](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=111771) and [Drain Life](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=234153).
  * [Pact of Gluttony](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91434)
    * Massive for survivability since it alters your [Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=6262) to [Demonic Healthstone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452930), which is useable multiple times per combat
  * [Blight of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136738) or [Blight of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136108) depending on the boss you're fighting. [Blight of Weakness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136108) be used as a defensive cooldown for your tank. [Blight of Tongues](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136738) can be very beneficial for slowing down important casts.

### How do my [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) work?

As a **Destruction Warlock** , your [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) behave differently compared to Affliction or Demonology. You don't generate 1 full [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) per cast, but rather Soul Shard Fragments.

10 Soul Shard Fragments build 1 full [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) and all your spells generate a different amount of Soul Shard Fragments, which is also influenced by potential Critical Strikes.

* [Immolate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348)
    * Generates 1 Soul Shard Fragment each DoT tick with a 50% to generate an additional one on Critical Strikes.
  * [Incinerate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=29722)
    * Generates 2 Soul Shard Fragments and an additional one on Critical Strikes baseline. Generates additional Shards with [Diabolic Embers](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91481)
  * [Conflagrate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=17962)
    * Generates 5 Soul Shard Fragments.
  * [Soul Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134221)
    * Generates 1 full [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985).
  * [Avatar of Destruction](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136833)
    * Generates 1 Soul Shard Fragment every second for 8 seconds.
  * [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91502)
    * Generates 1 Soul Shard Fragment every second for 30 seconds.
    * As [Diabolist](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123385) it breaks down into 2 smaller Infernals after 30 seconds due to [Abyssal Dominion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117428) which also generate Soul Shard Fragments  for another 10 seconds.
  * *[Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91493) / [Mayhem](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91494)
    * These are technically not generators but enhance [Incinerate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=29722), [Conflagrate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=17962), and [Soul Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134221) on successful duplicates, doubling the amount Soul Shard Fragments generated, even if the afflicted [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91493) target is immune.

### Maximizing your [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=80240)

To maximize your damage while [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=80240) is active you need to spend as many casts on [Chaos Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=116858) or [Rain of Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=5740) as possible. For this to happen you should enter the [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=80240) window with as many of the following conditions as possible:

* A high amount of [Soul Shards](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) or [Soul Shard](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=246985) income.
  * [Conflagrate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=17962) should be close to max stacks.
  * Refresh [Immolates](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=348) / [Wither](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117437) so they don't drop off during your [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=80240) window.

    * Dispels Magic and Bleed debuffs. Has been useful in the past on some bosses with Bleeds.
  * [Rocket Jump](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=69070) ___\-- Goblin___
    * Do a small jump in the direction your character is facing.
    * While you're in the [Rocket Jump](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=69070) animation you are placed on a global cooldown until your character lands.
  * [Berserking](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=26297) ___\-- Troll___
    * Strong DPS racial but lines up poorly with [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91502).
    * Reduce the duration of movement-impairing effects by 20%. This was only useful once in the recent history of progression raiding but is still worth noting.
  * [Blood Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=33702) ______\-- Orc___ ___
    * Strong racial for damage because of both [Command](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=21563) and [Blood Fury](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=33702) which lines up with [Summon Infernal](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91502).
    * 20% reduced stun duration on you which can be useful in some situations.
  * [Spatial Rift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=256948) _\-- Void Elf_
    * Spawn a shadow traveling into your facing direction, and activate it again to teleport to it.
    * Maximum range of 100 yards.

**Q: Which Pet do i use?**

**A:** In single-target fights where your pet does not need to switch it's target a lot [Summon Succubus](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=712) else use [Summon Imp](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=688).

**Q: When do i use [Mayhem](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91494) over [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91493)?**

**A:** Use [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91493) when it's beneficial to have [Havoc](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91493) at an exact moment in the fight. [Mayhem](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=91494) for more overall damage potential.
