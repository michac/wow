## Stat priority

The maxroll captures publish the secondary stat order only as an opaque
priority-import embed (`IoAJFMAIxQCKBEQABA`, identical in raid and M+), not as a
readable order. The one explicit textual guidance:

- **Item level first:** "Higher Item level items are better in most scenarios."
  A static stat priority is just a starting point — **sim your own gear**
  (Simcraft/Raidbots) for real upgrade decisions (maxroll).
- **Haste:** sims poorly on paper but is a great stat for multi-target — it
  smooths gameplay, adds [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920)
  charges (shorter recharge), aids Fury generation, and fits more abilities into
  [Demonic](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=213410) windows (maxroll).
- All secondary stats are subject to **diminishing returns**.

The exact secondary ordering (e.g. Crit/Haste/Mastery/Vers) is **not** in the
capture text. @verify-ingame — decode the priority embed or re-sim.

### Tertiary stats
- **Avoidance** — great for reducing AoE damage intake.
- **Leech** — extra self-healing through damage dealt.
- **Speed** — niche but proven useful; eases certain mechanics.

## Embellishments & crafted gear

- **2x [Arcanoweave Lining](https://www.wowhead.com/item=240167)** is the best combination.
- [Darkmoon Sigil: Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/item=245876) — can only be
  crafted on weapons; if you craft a weapon, put it there and ideally combine
  with another embellishment.
- **Honorable mention:** [Signet of Azerothian Blessings](https://www.wowhead.com/item=241140),
  and [Stabilizing Gemstone Bandolier](https://www.wowhead.com/item=251490)
  (combined with the Signet).
- **Remaining Sparks:** crafted items are 285 ilvl and regular items 289 at max
  ilvl, so it's a small loss to equip crafted items outside your 2x
  embellishments unless you lack high-ilvl gear in that slot (maxroll).
