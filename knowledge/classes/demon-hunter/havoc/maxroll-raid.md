### Apex Talents

**Apex talents** are a new feature in Midnight that extend each Spec Talent Tree. You can allocate 4 talent points to reap the full benefits of these special talents.

* The first talent point unlocks Rank 1.
  * The second and third talent point unlocks and enhances Rank 2.
  * The fourth point unlocks Rank 3 of the **Apex Talent**.

As **Havoc Demon Hunter** , you have access to [Eternal Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=110427), which empowers your [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013), lowers the cooldown and increases the target cap of [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965), and gives you a guaranteed reset of your [Blade Dance](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=188499) after casting [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013).

* **Rank 1** causes [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965) to empower your next [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013), doubling it's damage and increasing it's area of effect.
  * **Rank 2** gives [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965) 15/30 sec reduced cooldown, makes it do 15/30% more damage and it's DoT effect can apply to 2/4 extra targets.
  * **Rank 3** Increases [Blade Dance](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=188499) damage by 20%, and will cause it to reset it's own cooldown the first time it's cast after fully channeling [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013).

### Hero Talent overview

* The [Fel-Scarred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123329) hero talent tree is generally more passive than [Aldrachi Reaver](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123330). In addition to it's normal benefits, casting [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=191427) transforms [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013) into [Abyssal Gaze](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452497) and [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920) into [Consuming Fire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452487) empowering them, causing them to trigger a [Demonsurge](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117514) the first time they are cast.
  * At the same time, the tree has talents like [Monster Rising](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452414), [Burning Blades](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452408) and [Enduring Torment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=452410) that significantly increase your output out of [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=191427).
  * [Fel-Scarred](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123329) is generally considered easier to play compared to [Aldrachi Reaver](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123330), which is something worth considering since it might give you more room to focus on more important stuff than damage, such as mechanics and living.
  * Make sure to put [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920) and [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013) on cooldown before using [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=191427) as [Violent Transformation](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117509) resets those abilities.

    * Does massive damage in a frontal cone and causes [Chaos Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=162794) and [Blade Dance](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=188499) to deal additional Chaos damage to affected enemies.

### Dead or Alive

The most important part of doing damage and being a useful part of your Raid group is staying alive. With movement being a core part of your damage rotation you NEED to always be aware of your surroundings. Do not [Fel Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=195072) or [Vengeful Retreat](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198793) into frontal abilities or area of effect zones.

You cannot do damage while you are dead and neither can you stop abilities from going through.

Being able to execute the perfect rotation isn't going to help you if you are lying dead on the floor or if you need externals/healing that your group would need otherwise.

### [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920)

When you have [Ragefire](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=390197) \+ [A Fire Inside](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=427775) talented it is important to manage your [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920) charges properly as it is the biggest portion of your damage breakdown in AoE scenarios. When you are about to overcap on charges but you don't have [Fel Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=195072) charges ready to spend on [Inertia](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=427640) uptime just use your [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920) charges and ignore [Inertia](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=427640).

**Never** overcap on [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920) charges, no matter what!

### [Initiative](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388108) Windows

Another important part of your damage is managing your [Initiative](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388108) windows properly. Remember that every single hostile enemy procs [Initiative](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388108) and [Vengeful Retreat](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198793) also resets it on EVERY hostile enemy.

You can optimize this by staying out of range of some enemies to proc[Initiative](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=388108) again later.

### Spreading [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965)

When casting [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965) it is important to make sure you afflict as many enemies as possible with the DoT component of the ability. This can be tricky since it is applied to enemies that are in your path when you charge with [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965). A trick to help with this is that you can actually still apply this DoT after the cast/charge of [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965) is over. This means that you can cast [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965), and after it's done you can walk through enemies to apply this dot component to them, and if you are quick you can even cast [Fel Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=195072) AFTER [The Hunt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=370965), and it will still apply the DoT to the enemies you [Fel Rush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=195072) through.

### **Haste** ?!

Even though **Haste** Sims pretty poorly on paper, it is a great stat for multi target scenarios as it smooths your gameplay out by a lot.

**Haste** increases the amount of [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258920) charges you have as it decreases the recharge time and helps with Fury generation as well as helping you fit more abilities into your [Demonic](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=213410) windows.

    * Collective Anguish, Demonic, and The Hunt have moved to the Havoc talent tree (was class talents).
    * Several talents have changed positions in the talent tree.
    * The following talents and skills have been removed:
      * Fel Barrage
      * Fel Eruption
      * Looks Can Kill
      * Improved Fel Rush
      * Insatiable Hunger
      * Netherwalk
      * Restless Hunter

**Q: Is it worth holding my [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112939) for X?**

A: It is almost never worth holding your Eye Beam for specific points unless you hold it for less than 3-4 seconds. You are better off just using your [Eye Beam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198013), [Essence Break](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=258860) and [Vengeful Retreat](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198793) on cooldown.
