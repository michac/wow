# Vengeance Demon Hunter — Abilities (Midnight S1)

## Overview

Vengeance is the Demon Hunter **tank** spec — a builder/spender melee that
turns damage taken and dealt into self-sustain. Two resources drive it:

- **Fury** (0–120) — the spender currency. Generated by Fracture, Immolation
  Aura, Sigil of Flame, Felblade; spent by Soul Cleave, Spirit Bomb, Fel
  Devastation.
- **Soul Fragments** (0–5 active, more "in-flight") — physical orbs spawned by
  builders and several cooldowns. Consumed by **Spirit Bomb** and **Soul
  Cleave**, each consumed fragment adding damage and self-healing.

Core loop: **Fracture** to make Fury + 2 fragments → dump into **Spirit Bomb**
(AoE, at high fragment count) or **Soul Cleave** (ST / healing), interleaving
**Immolation Aura**, **Sigil of Flame**, **Fiery Brand** and **Metamorphosis**
for damage and mitigation, while keeping **Demon Spikes** up as active
mitigation. The two Midnight hero trees bolt a distinct sub-game on top:
**Aldrachi Reaver** (a Reaver's Glaive empower cycle) or **Annihilator** (a
Voidfall-stack / Untethered-Rage burst engine). See `rotation.md` / `builds.md`.

> **Midnight changes to flag:** **Spirit Bomb now carries a short (~25s,
> haste-reduced) cooldown** in Midnight (the APL references
> `cooldown.spirit_bomb`) rather than being a pure fragment-dump — verify the
> exact value in-game. **Metamorphosis** is down to a **2-minute** cooldown and
> makes Fracture generate **+15 Fury** while active. **Annihilator** is a
> Midnight-new hero tree (it replaces The War Within's Fel-scarred). @verify-ingame
