Vengeance is a **tank**; gearing is survivability-first. Haste is the top
secondary in both raid and M+ (damage + cooldown reduction on [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795)
generators and [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720), i.e. mitigation uptime). The rest of the
priority splits by content — see below. **Always sim your own gear**; a static
stat priority is only a starting point.

## Stat priority

**Raid** (maxroll, sim-verify): Haste is the most sought-after stat (damage +
CDR on soul-fragment generators and [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720)). **Versatility is slightly
better than Critical Strike** for survivability in raid — more consistent, and
many bosses have tank busters / unparryable hits, so crit's parry value is
reduced. Prefer Versatility for progress raiding.

**Mythic+** (maxroll, sim-verify): Haste again top. **Critical Strike is highly
valued in M+** — it boosts damage substantially and increases parry, which is a
large amount of damage reduction when taking many melee hits.

**Tertiary stats** (from the capture):
- **Avoidance** — reduces AoE damage taken; heavy diminishing returns starting at
  ~20% (2000 rating), no hard cap. **Best tertiary for raid** min-maxing survival.
- **Leech** — instantly returns health from outgoing damage/healing (1% Leech =
  1% of damage & healing returned). **Best tertiary for M+**; also better than
  Avoidance in raid on fights with many long-lived, hard-to-tank adds.
- **Speed** — movement speed.

All secondary stats are subject to [diminishing returns](https://maxroll.gg/wow/resources/stat-diminishing-returns).

## Embellishments & crafted gear

- **1x** [Loa Worshiper's Band](https://www.wowhead.com/item=251513) — best overall choice of stat budget.
- [Stabilizing Gemstone Bandolier](https://www.wowhead.com/item=251490)

**Remaining Sparks:** crafted items are 285 item level vs 289 for regular items
at max ilvl, so it's a small loss to equip crafted gear outside your 2x
embellishments unless you lack high-ilvl gear in that slot (maxroll, sim-verify).
