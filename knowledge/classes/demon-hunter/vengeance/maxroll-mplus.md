### Apex Talents

**Apex talents** are a new feature in Midnight that extend each Spec Talent Tree. You can allocate 4 talent points to reap the full benefits of these special talents.

* The first talent point unlocks Rank 1.
  * The second and third talent point unlocks and enhances Rank 2.
  * The fourth point unlocks Rank 3 of the **Apex Talent**.

As **Vengeance Demon Hunter** , you have access to [Untethered Rage](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=110425), which grants a chance per soul fragment consumed by [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477) and [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=247454) to give you a free [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) use.

**Rank 2** increases the damage of [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477) and [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=247454) and grants them a chance to consume an extra soul. While **Rank 3** enables that every consumed soul that did not trigger a free [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) gives you a stacking Agility buff and increases the chance of proccing a free [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827).

### Core Changes

Following you receive a list of meaningful class changes going from The War Within into Midnight to keep you updated. For a more detailed look of **all** changes, check out our **Changes This Patch** section.

* **Spec Tree**
    * [Sigil of Spite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112894)
      * Big AoE damage cooldown that grants souls and was moved from Class Tree to Spec tree.
    * [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112907)
      * [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112907) was turned from an AoE soul spender to a small AoE cooldown that synergizes with [Soul Barrier](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112870) and grants you a big shield.
    * [Vengeful Beast](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112899)
      * Turns your [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) in an actual damage cooldown now, as it increases the damage of all your main rotational abilities by 20% inside of [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827).
    * [Soul Barrier](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112870)
      * Grants you a fancy shield whenever you press [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112907).

* **Class Tree**
    * [Demon Muzzle](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112838)
      * Decreases magic damage taken whenever you interrupt a spell.
    * [Final Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136501)
      * Significantly increases the damage of your [Fel Devastation](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112908).
    * [First In, Last Out](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117758)
      * [Infernal Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=189110) now grants you a small temporarily shield that decays over time.
    * [Burn It Out](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112837) & [Soul Cleanse](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136502)
      * Allows you to pick a choice node where [Immolation Aura](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=195447) either clears a curse or a disease effect of you.
    * [Wings of Wrath](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136500)
      * Insane talent that makes you go very fast while gliding.
  * **Removed**
    * [Demonic](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=321453)
      * You no longer have the ability to enter [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) when casting [Fel Devastation](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112908) which turns this into a pure damage cooldown now.

* For average Mythic+ I'd normally recommend [Annihilator](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134253), since it is very easy to play and has a low skill ceiling and significantly helps with snap threat and making ur team happy.

**[Annihilator](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134253)**

* The [Annihilator](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134253) talent tree evolves around generating and spending [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135667) stacks:
    * [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) has a 35% chance to grant 1 stack of [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135667)
    * [Meteoric Fall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135664) makes using [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=227255) or [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477) after reaching 3 stacks consume all of them and call down 3 meteors that deal big AOE damage.
    * With [Catastrophe](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135671), the meteors additionally cause their targets to take 25% additional damage for 8 seconds.
    * [World Killer](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135660) reduces [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) cooldown by 10 seconds after every third meteor that is called down.
  * [Swift Erasure](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135672) and [Phase Shift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135670) increase haste and reduce damage taken by 2% for each active stack of [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135667).
    * [Final Hour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135663) makes this effect last for 8 seconds after being consumed.
  * This results in gameplay that revolves around constantly building [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135667) stacks with [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) and consuming them with [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=227255) or [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477).
    * While this happens naturally by doing normal rotation, it is important to consume [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135667) stacks as early as possible, to not overcap them.

**[Aldrachi Reaver](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123328)**

* Consuming 20 [Soul Fragments](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203981) or casting [Sigil of Spite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389815) converts your next [Throw Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204157) into [Reaver's Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442294).
    * This enhances your next [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) and [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477). The enhanced ability you cast first deals 10% increased damage, and the second deals 20% increased damage. 
    * Quite simply casting [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) > [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477) is the priority of empowerments after using [Reaver's Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442294). 
  * The second enhanced ability from using [Reaver's Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442294) shatters an additional [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795) through [Aldrachi Tactics](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117511), this allows you to get [Reaver's Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442294) back faster by consuming more [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795). 
  * [Reaver's Mark](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117500) is a debuff that causes the target to take 7% increased damage for 20 seconds from an enhanced [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) from [Reaver's Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442294). 
    * If cast after [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477), [Reaver's Mark](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117500) is increased to 14%. 
    * [Reaver's Mark](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117500) also stacks and can be increased from 7% to 14% by doing a second set of empowerments starting with [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) > [Soul Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=228477). 
    * Additionally while [Reaver's Mark](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117500) is up, [Wounded Quarry](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117494) gives your melee attacks additional damage and has a chance to shatter a [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795). 
  * [Unhindered Assault](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123047) gives [Vengeful Retreat](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112853) a way of generating **Fury** as it resets the cooldown of [Felblade](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=213241). 
  * [Incorruptible Spirit](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=123046) is extremely strong as it allows you to have incredible amounts of absorbs because you how many [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795) you currently consume. 
  * [Thrill of the Fight](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=117516) is a passive buff you get from consuming both enhancements from [Reaver's Glaive](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=442294) that gives 6% increased haste.

### Gameplay Altering Talents

Discover all talents that significantly alter your gameplay within the Spec and Class Talent trees. This section gives a concise overview of these talents and their applications but for a more detailed look check out the **[Rotation](http://maxroll.gg/wow/class-guides/vengeance-demon-hunter-raid-guide#rotation-header)** and **[Deep Dive](https://maxroll.gg/wow/class-guides/vengeance-demon-hunter-raid-guide#deep-dive-header)**  sections below.

#### Spec Tree

* [Frailty](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112893)
    * A debuff that makes you heal for 8% or 16% with [Soulcrush](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389985) of the damage done. This works exactly like Leech. 
  * [Charred Flesh](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=336639)
    * Gives [Fiery Brand](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204021) a lot higher uptime, making it very strong. 
  * [Fel Devastation](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=212084)
    * Big damage frontal cone ability that also heals you. 
  * [Sigil of Silence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=202137)
    * AoE silence in an 8 yard radius.
  * [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=227255)
    * Small 25 seconds AoE cooldown to generate snap threat that consumes souls.
    * Applies [Frailty](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=112893).
  * [Down in Flames](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389732)
    * Makes [Fiery Brand](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204021) a 45 second cooldown instead of 1 minute and gives it an additional charge. Damage reduction uptime from [Fiery Brand](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204021) becomes a lot higher with this, allowing you to cycle defensives more easily.

#### Class Tree

* [Soul Rending](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204909)
    * A 2 point node that gives 5% Leech per point and 5% additional Leech when [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) is active.
  * [Chaos Nova](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=179057)
    * A very potent 45 seconds cooldown AoE stun.
  * [Sigil of Misery](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=202140)
    * Your AoE disorientation that CC's mobs for 15 seconds. 
    * Also acts as a stop, or interrupt by canceling a mob's cast.

### Defensive Cooldowns

* [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827)
    * [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) is your biggest defensive cooldown, and you should learn to plan around [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) usage to have a smoother time mitigating incoming damage.

* [Fiery Brand](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204021)
    * [Fiery Brand](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204021) gives you 40% damage reduction while applying a DoT to your target. Normally, you want to use this spell on cooldown as it does decent damage, and can buff your other fire damage spells with [Fiery Demise](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=212818). However, hold this spell if there is a tank buster or mechanic that does a lot of damage, and you need to save extra defensives to survive. With talents, it can be extended through [Charred Flesh](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=336639) to last even longer than usual and have an additional charge as well as a shorter cooldown with [Down in Flames](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389732).

* [Darkness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=196718)
    * [Darkness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=196718) lasts 8 seconds and causes all damage you or an ally takes while inside the darkness to have a 15% chance of being negated completely. This is not great compared to large single hits, but it is good for numerous small hits, such as melee attacks. If you're not saving it for something in particular, you should go ahead and use it almost on cooldown because of the defensive benefits.

### Offensive Cooldowns

* [Sigil of Spite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389815)
    * [Sigil of Spite](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389815) deals a moderate amount of damage and generates 3 [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795), allowing you to cast [Spirit Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=227255) more frequently.

### Sigils

* Sigils are **Demon Hunter** specific abilities that are placed on the ground and then have a delay of 2 seconds before having an effect within an 8-yard radius. 
    * Talenting into [Quickened Sigils](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=209281) brings that delay down to 1 second. 
    * They generate 1 [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204255) with [Soul Sigils](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=395446). 
    * [Chains of Anger](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389715) increases the duration of your sigils by 2 seconds and their radius by 2 yards. 
    * [Cycle of Binding](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=389718) reduces the cooldown of all your sigils by 15%.

### Cycling defensives on **Vengea**nce Demon Hunter**

* **Vengea**nce Demon Hunter's** active mitigation [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720) has very high uptime, meaning that as **Vengea**nce Demon Hunter** , you can utilize macros and bind [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720) into your [Fracture](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=225919) ability without any defensive loss.
  * For cycling defensives, while [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720) is our most available active defensive, players should be trying to maximize the uptime of [Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=187827) > [Fiery Brand](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=204021) > [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720) in that order.
    * This does mean that you end up overlapping and layering [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720) with other defensives. The main goal is to make sure that you're not capping [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720) charges either. 
  * This type of gameplay sets **Vengea**nce Demon Hunter** apart from other tanks since it needs to be done frequently in high-level content, as taking damage with no defensive cooldown can spell disaster.

### Boss Tips

#### Arcanotron Custos

* [Repulsing Slam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=474496) is the tank mechanic of the fight. It deals magic damage and knocks you back.
  * When the boss reaches 100 energy, he starts casting [Refueling Protocol](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=474345). Make sure to collect any arcane balls flying towards him during this cast in order to prevent [Arcane Empowerment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=474407) from happening.

#### Seranel Sunlash

* This boss buffs himself with [Hastening Ward](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1248689). This buff increases his attack speed and makes his auto attacks deal additional magic damage.
  * Make sure to let your group clean [Runic Mark](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1224801) first by going into [Suppression Zone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1225015), before doing it yourself.
  * Whenever the boss casts [Wave of Silence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1225193), make sure to be inside of a [Suppression Zone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1225015) to not get hit by the [Wave of Silence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1225193).

#### Gemellus

* The boss will [Triplicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1223847) at the start of the fight, creating 2 clones of himself.
    * The clones do not need to be tanked and do not cast any abilities.
    * The clones and the boss have shared health. Keep the boss close to his clones to enable efficient cleave.

#### **Degentrius**

* The boss will cast [Hulking Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1280113) on you, which is the tank mechanic.
    * It deals a large amount of shadow damage and applies [Umbral Splinters](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1284627) \- a dispellable shadow DOT.
    * Upon getting dispelled, you will leave a zone of [Stygian Ichor](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1284628) underneath that does ticking damage while standing in it. 
    * Try to get dispelled inside of an existing [Stygian Ichor](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1284628) to minimize the area denial.
  * Every now and then, there will be a [Unstable Void Essence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1215087) to soak in your quadrant. Make sure to stand in it and don't let it be unsoaked!

### Reasons for Stats

Haste is always the most sought after Stat as it gives a high amount of damage, as well as cdr on [Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795) generating abilities and [Demon Spikes](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203720), which gives a lot of survivability. In Mythic+ Critical Strike is usually very sought after as it increases your damage by a substantial amount and increases your parry, which when you are taking a lot of melee hits, equals a large amount of Damage Reduction.

All secondary stats are affected by **diminishing returns**. **[Click here](https://maxroll.gg/wow/resources/stat-diminishing-returns)** to learn more!

### Tertiary Stats

* **Avoidance** \- Slightly reduces damage taken from area of effect attacks. This can be certain tank abilities a boss does, as well as trash. Starts diminishing returns heavily at 20% (2000), but it has no cap. 
  * **Leech** \- Causes a player to instantly return health from both outgoing damage and outgoing heals. Health returned is equal to your amount of Leech. For Example; 1 % of Leech gives health equal to 1% of damage and healing done. 
  * **Speed** \- Increases movement speed.

In conclusion, **Leech** is what you should go for as a tank in Mythic+ in order to min-max survivability as it gives you the greatest benefit in Mythic+ over the other two tertiary stats.

**Q: How to get better at tanking?**

The biggest tip I can give is to always figure out why you died in a given scenario and how you can improve. Tanking is tough, with constant opportunities to better use your externals, rotation, and defensives. Recording your gameplay and reviewing Warcraft Logs are great methods. Being good at tanking requires cognitive flexibility—focus on what you could have done better instead of blaming others. This mindset is more rewarding and healthier for becoming a better tank.
