### Apex Talents

**Apex talents** are a new feature in Midnight that extend each Spec Talent Tree. You can allocate 4 talent points to reap the full benefits of these special talents.

* The first talent point unlocks Rank 1.
  * The second and third talent point unlocks and enhances Rank 2.
  * The fourth point unlocks Rank 3 of the **Apex Talent**.

As **Devourer Demon Hunter** , you have access to [Midnight](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=110416), which enables [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) to be an actually pressable button, the first Rank makes it so [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) always crits, which synergizes very well with talents like [Calamitous](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=136691).

**Rank 2** increases all cosmic damage you deal by 6% and increases the damage of [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) furthermore since it always critically strikes anyway  
  
**Rank 3** enables the use of [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) as soon as you enter [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282) and grants you an additional 5 souls when you enter [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282).

  * [Swift Erasure](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135672), [Phase Shift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135670) increase haste and reduce damage taken by 2% capped at 3 stacks.

This especially tends to happen whenever you enter [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282), as people tend to [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1213649) to get it on cooldown as soon as possible.

### Gameplay Altering Talents

Discover all talents that significantly alter your gameplay within the Spec and Class Talent trees. This section gives a concise overview of these talents and their applications but for a more detailed look check out the Rotation and **Deep Dive** sections below.

#### Spec Tree

* [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282)
    * Entering [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282) after collecting 50 souls (35 if talented into [Soul Glutton](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=134331)) enhance your Consume and Reap, also Fury is slowly being drained the longer you stay in [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282), [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1242157) now grants fury instead of spending it.
  * [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281)
    * Every 30 souls that are collected during [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282) grants access to a cast of [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) doing massive damage to your main target and around it, additionally fury drain is significantly slowed during it.

* [Moment of Craving](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=133106)
    * Resets your [Reap](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226019) after casting [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1213649).
  * [Focused Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=133110)
    * [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1213649) deals significantly more damage if you only hit one target, avoid hitting off targets if you do not want to.
  * [Eradicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132287)
    * [Reap](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226019) turns into [Eradicate](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132287) after fully channeling a [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1213649), massively increasing the damage and adding a frontal AoE component to it.
  * [Emptiness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=133512)
    * Allowing you to stay [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282) significantly longer and increases your haste with every soul you collect.

#### Class Tree

* [Darkness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=196718)
    * This group-wide defensive cooldown creates a zone of [Darkness](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=196718) at the **Devourer Demon Hunter's** location, granting all allies within it a 15% chance to avoid all damage from incoming attacks.

#### Hero Talents

* [Voidfall](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=135667)
    * Consume has a 35% chance to grant you a stack of voidfall, after 3 stacks your next reap does massive aoe damage around it.

### Dead or Alive

The most important part of doing damage and being a useful part of your group is staying alive. With movement being a core part of your damage rotation you NEED to always be aware of your surroundings. Do not [Shift](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1234796), [Voidblade](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245412) or [Vengeful Retreat](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=198793) into frontal abilities or area of effect zones.

You cannot do damage while you are dead and neither can you stop abilities from going through.

Being able to execute the perfect rotation isn't going to help you if you are lying dead on the floor or if you need externals/healing that your tank would need otherwise.

### Making the most out of[Soul Fragment](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=203795)'s

Always remember that Soul Fragment management is a crucial part of maximizing your DPS as **Devourer Demon Hunter** , staying in [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282) for as long as possible should be your goal.  
THIS BEING SAID: There are 3 ways of collecting souls,

1. Casting [Reap](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1226019)
  2. Auto collecting by too many being on the floor
  3. Running over them  
People tend to forget the second and third way which makes them waste massive amounts of fury and overcap a ton in [Void Metamorphosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132282) wasting significant amounts of uptime.  
Even if it sounds alluring that your reap or eradicate damage is increased by the amount of souls you collect, it is sometimes smart to pick them up by hand to squeeze in another [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) faster to not waste cooldown of [Void Ray](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132278) or squeezing in another [Collapsing Star](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=132281) at the end of meta while spamming [Devour](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1217610)'s and running over souls to gain fury and not waste globals on a non soul spawning ability like reap.  
It also takes some time for the souls to hit you after casting reap as they are collected, walking towards them can help.

### Boss Tips

#### Arcanotron Custos

* Pay attention to the energy of the boss since it takes increased damage whenever it fully depletes it and don't spend offensive cooldowns right before that.
    * It takes around ~50 seconds each time to deplete the energy from full to 0.
  * Be careful whenever you are rooted by [Ethereal Shackles](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1214038) and be ready to press a defensive cooldown or a health potion if needed since not everyone can be dispelled at once.

#### Seranel Sunlash

* Plan your defensive cooldowns whenever you are cleansing the [Runic Marks](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1225792).

#### Gemellus

* It's very important to know that, due to [Synaptic Nexus](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1223936), everything shares health, which means dealing maximum amount of damage, cleaving as much as possible, is the best way to defeat the boss.
  * Additionally, don't forget to trigger the [Neural Link](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1253707) and touch the Gemellus that you are linked to, this way you will remove a giant absorb shield from the boss and will greatly speed up the encounter.
  * Be careful whenever you are afflicted by a [Cosmic Sting](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1284958) and press a defensive cooldown if needed.

#### Degentrius

* Spread around with at least one person covering one section of the room.
  * Position yourself that way so you can see your whole section and be ready to soak the [Unstable Void Essence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1215087) whenever it's needed.

### Trash Tips

* Important abilities to interrupt in this dungeon are:
    * [Pyroblast](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1254294) from **Blazing Pyromancer**.
    * [Arcane Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=468962) and [Polymorph](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=468966) from **Arcane Magister**.
    * [Shadow Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1248327) from **Dreadful Voidwalker**.
    * [Terror Wave](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1264693) from **Void Terror**.
      * Very dangerous cast and should never let it go through.

* Dangerous debuffs to be wary of:
    * [Void Bomb](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1248138) from **Devouring Tyrant**.
      * Inflicts shadow damage to all players hit once it expires. Every unit hit have their damage done increased and absorbs healing.

* Useful debuffs to be wary of:
    * [Energy Release](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1254595) from **Brightscale Wyrm**.
      * Applies a stacking damage and healing increase on players upon dying.

### Boss Tips

#### Muro'Jin and Nekraxx

* Keep in mind that you want to kill both bosses roughly at the same time due to [Revive Pet](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249789) and [Bestial Wrath](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249948).
  * Spread around the boss to avoid cleaving too much with [Barrage](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1260643) but keep a [Freezing Trap](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1260731) nearby to have a way to deal with [Carrion Swoop](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249479).

#### Vordaza

* Press a defensive cooldown during [Necrotic Convergence](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1250708).
  * Decide within your group how you want to deal with **Unstable Phantoms**. 
  * Don't stay too far from the boss or it will be too hard to dodge the [Unmake](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1252054) frontal.

#### Rak'tul, Vessel of Souls

* Try your best to position the **Soulbind Totems** from [Crush Souls](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1252676) as close as possible. You can see the order of leaps slightly ahead of time, ideally you can position all **Totems** so close that all of them will be cleaved from the boss.
  * Keep in mind that you are getting a huge damage buff during the fight, roughly 1:30 into the encounter, so plan your offensive cooldowns accordingly.

  * Use a defensive cooldown whenever you are targeted with [Splattering Spew](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=472745).
  * Look out for [Heaving Yank](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=472795) and don't be in the way if you are not the main target of it.

Don't forget to group up right after it to not get feared from [Intimidating Shout](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1253026).

* Spread around the boss but not too far to have an easier time dealing with a [Bolt Gale](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=474528) frontal.

**Q: How do i avoid missing Collapsing Stars?**

A: Make sure to pre-plan accordingly, since you lose all your souls if you cancel your cast.  
1\. Position properly before the cast, move out of any swirly and make sure u dont have to move  
2\. Target the highest or atleast a high health target so it does not die before u finish the cast  
3\. !!!DO NOT SPAM THE BUTTON!!! as it tends to bug it out for ping reasons

You rather lose a global than cancelling a Collapsing star cast by mistake.
