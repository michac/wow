# Devourer Demon Hunter — gearing (Midnight S1)

> **Split out of `builds.md` (2026-07-14).** Talents/loadouts/hero-tree live in

> slot where **effect > ilvl**.

## Stat priority

**Mastery > Haste > Crit ≈ Versatility** (Method 12.0.7): "Mastery and Haste
provide significantly more value than Critical Strike and Versatility; prioritize
Mastery into Haste." The **Void-Scarred melee** variant shifts toward **Crit**
(and Haste weaker).

- **Higher item level wins in most slots** — a static priority is only a starting
  point; sim your own gear on Raidbots for close calls (maxroll, sim-verify).
- All secondary stats are subject to **diminishing returns** (maxroll).
- **Haste caveat (raid & M+):** it "sims pretty poorly on paper" but is a great
  stat for **multi-target** because it smooths the rotation a lot (maxroll). Raid
  and M+ share the same maxroll stat-priority import; no divergence noted between
  them.
- **Tertiaries** (maxroll): **Avoidance** (reduces AoE damage taken), **Leech**
  (self-healing), **Speed** (niche but useful for mechanics).

use **Bloodmallet** for ilvl-scaled sims. Method's called-out best trinket is

## Embellishments & crafted gear

- **1x [Loa Worshiper's Band](https://www.wowhead.com/item=251513)** — best overall choice of stat budget (maxroll).
- [Stabilizing Gemstone Bandolier](https://www.wowhead.com/item=251490).
- **Remaining Sparks:** crafted items are **285 ilvl** vs regular items at **289**
  at max ilvl, so it's a small loss to equip crafted items outside your 2x
  embellishments unless you lack high-ilvl gear in that slot (maxroll).
