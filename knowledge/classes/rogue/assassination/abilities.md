# Assassination Rogue — abilities (Midnight S1)

## Overview

Assassination is the **poison-and-bleed** Rogue spec: a slow, methodical
damage-over-time playstyle built on maintaining two bleeds (**Garrote**,
**Rupture**), keeping the **Envenom** buff up to raise poison-application
chance, and ramping burst through the **Deathmark + Kingsbane** cooldown pair.
Method's framing: "a slow and methodical spec focused on mastery over
resources, rather than quick reflexes." Damage is mostly passive (poison DoTs
and bleeds tick on their own); the active rotation feeds those DoTs.

- **Resource system:** **Energy** (0–100+, regenerates continuously; spent by
  builders) plus **Combo Points** (0–5, up to 7 with Deeper Stratagem;
  generated by builders, consumed by finishers).
- **Builders** (spend energy, make CP): Mutilate, Ambush, Fan of Knives,
  Garrote, Crimson Tempest (reworked into a generator in Midnight).
- **Finishers** (spend CP): Envenom (primary spender), Rupture (bleed),
  Kidney Shot (stun), Crimson Tempest historically — see the rework note below.
- **Two hero trees:** **Deathstalker** (the simc default profile; revolves
  around Deathstalker's Mark → Darkest Night empowered Envenoms) and
  **Fatebound** (Method's recommended pick for quality-of-life — a passive
  coin-flip package on 5+ CP finishers). See `builds.md`.

> **Midnight changes to know (Method, 2026-06-16):** Indiscriminate Carnage
> and the baseline **Shiv** were removed / reworked, and Master Assassin's
> stealth effects were removed. **Crimson Tempest was reworked** from a pure
> AoE finisher into a **combo-point generator that spreads bleeds** — the simc
> APL now casts it from `actions.generate`. **Shiv** returns only via the
> **Toxic Stiletto** class talent (a thrown poison applicator), used at 6 CP
> when Darkest Night is available. @verify-ingame (Shiv/Crimson Tempest exact
> reworked behaviour)
