🛡 Staying alive

Survival Instincts · 6s
−60% damage taken. Your "oh no" button — works in cat.
Barkskin
Smaller wall, no form needed, doesn't break anything. You talented Improved Barkskin + Oakskin — it's beefy now.
Frenzied Regeneration
Heals 32% over 3s (Bear Form). Your Well-Honed Instincts auto-casts it free when you drop below 40% (90s ICD).
Regrowth + passives
Drop form to heal between pulls. Aessina's Renewal also auto-heals 10% when something hits you for >12% (30s ICD).
Bear Form
Still your panic tank-suit: way more health + armor. Bear up, Frenzied Regen, walk away with dignity.
Rebirth
The battle res. Usable in combat — in any group content, this is why people love you.

✋ Control & interrupts

Skull Bash · 15s
Your interrupt — it's also a short charge. Kick the caster, lock the school 5s. Bind this well.
Maim · 30s
Finisher stun — up to 5s at 5 CP. Spend points on this instead of Bite when a mob needs to stop existing politely.
Incapacitating Roar · 30s
AoE 3s incap (breaks on damage) — your "everyone calm down" button when a pull goes wrong.
Typhoon · 30s
Frontal knockback + 50% slow. Works in every form. Cliffs exist; you do the math.
Ursol's Vortex
Drops a vortex that slows and sucks fleeing enemies back in. Pairs hilariously with Typhoon.
Entangling Roots
Still here, still roots. Caster form (or macro it) — great on the second mob of a bad pull.

🏃 Movement & misc

Tiger Dash · 45s
+200% speed decaying over 5s — your Dash got an upgrade. Also: Feline Swiftness has you 15% faster all the time.
Stampeding Roar · 2m
+60% speed for everyone within 15 yds for 8s. Group utility — people remember the druid who roars.
Shapeshifting tech
Shifting forms still breaks roots & snares. Travel Form is instant outdoors and flies where flight is unlocked.
Prowl
Stealth between pulls; every pull should start with a stun-Rake out of it. Sudden Ambush also procs free, empowered stealth-attacks mid-fight.
Remove Corruption · 8s
Dispels Curses and Poisons off friendlies (including you). Soothe (10s) strips enemy enrages.
Mark of the Wild
Buff it, keep it up. Your Gift of the Wild makes it twice as effective on yourself.
