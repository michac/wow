# Balance Druid — abilities (Midnight S1)

## Overview

- **Resource:** **Astral Power** (0–100, cap raised by talents). Generated by
  builders (Wrath, Starfire, Moonfire/Sunfire ticks, the Moon chain, Fury of
  Elune, Nature's Balance passive) and consumed by spenders (Starsurge,
  Starfall, Wild/Sunseeker Mushroom).
- **Hero trees (Midnight):** **Keeper of the Grove** (raid / single-target —
  Treants + Harmony of the Grove burst, Force of Nature centric) and **Elune's
  Chosen** (Mythic+ / sustained AoE — Fury of Elune centric, Lunation cooldown
  reduction, Atmospheric Exposure). See `builds.md`.
- **Playstyle:** a strict-priority caster played in and out of **Eclipse**.
  **Midnight reworked Eclipse from a passive into an activated button:** your
  last builder sets its state — casting **Wrath** arms **Solar Eclipse**,
  casting **Starfire** arms **Lunar Eclipse** — and you press **Eclipse** to
  enter that state, empowering the matching filler while keeping Moonfire +
  Sunfire rolling and stacking cooldowns (Celestial Alignment/Incarnation +
  Convoke + Force of Nature + Fury of Elune) every ~2 min.
