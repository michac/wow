# Marksmanship Hunter — Abilities (Midnight S1)

## Overview

- **Hero trees (S1):** **Sentinel** (the S1 default — best in nearly all PvE;
  replaces Spotter's Mark with **Sentinel's Mark**, adds **Moonlight Chakram**
  and **Lunar Storm**) and **Dark Ranger** (defensively stronger, preferred on
  pure single-target fights; replaces Kill Shot with **Black Arrow** and adds
  **Withering Fire → Wailing Arrow**). See `builds.md`.
- **Resource: Focus** (0–100, passive regen). **Steady Shot** is the primary
  Focus builder; **Aimed Shot**, **Arcane Shot**, and **Multi-Shot** spend it.
  Rapid Fire is a channel that is Focus-neutral/generating.
- **Playstyle:** a ranged burst-caster. Weave the hard-cast **Aimed Shot**
  (2 charges) with instant **Precise Shots** consumers (Arcane/Multi-Shot),
  keep **Rapid Fire** and **Aimed Shot** on cooldown, and pool everything into
  the **Trueshot** burst window every ~90s–2min. **Aimed Shot is the only cast
  you must stand still for**; almost everything else is instant or a channel.

## Core rotational mechanics (buffs/procs, not buttons)

- **Precise Shots** — generated by Aimed Shot; empowers and reduces the Focus
  cost of the next **Arcane Shot / Multi-Shot**. Don't overcap.
- **Trick Shots** — Multi-Shot on **3+ targets** (or **Volley**) grants it;
  makes **Aimed Shot** and **Rapid Fire** cleave to nearby enemies. The AoE
  enabler. (Aspect of the Hydra instead lets shots hit 2 targets without setup.)
- **Sentinel's Mark / Spotter's Mark** — an Aimed-Shot damage stack gained from
  spending Precise Shots (Sentinel = Sentinel's Mark, Dark Ranger = Spotter's).
- **Lock and Load** — Explosive Shot proc → a free/instant Aimed Shot.
- **Bulletstorm** (Sentinel) — Rapid Fire builds stacks, each +1% Aimed Shot
  damage (max ~20).
- **Deathblow** (Dark Ranger) — proc (Trueshot, Wailing Arrow, Rapid Fire,
  Aimed Shot chance) that enables **Black Arrow** off the execute window.
