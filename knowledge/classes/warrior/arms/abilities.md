# Arms Warrior — Ability Inventory (Midnight S1)

## Overview

Arms is the two-handed melee DPS spec for Warrior. Its resource is **Rage**
(0–100/120), generated by auto-attacks, `Charge`, and several abilities, and
spent on `Mortal Strike`, `Slam`/`Heroic Strike`, `Execute`, and `Cleave`. The
playstyle is a **priority list built around the `Colossus Smash` window** — a
~45s debuff that ignores armor and amplifies damage — into which you funnel your
hardest hits (`Mortal Strike`, `Overpower`, `Avatar`, plus the hero-tree finisher
`Demolish` or a `Bladestorm`). `Overpower` empowers `Mortal Strike`, `Deep Wounds`
+ `Rend` are the bleeds, and `Sudden Death` grants free `Execute` procs outside the
execute phase.

**Hero trees (pick one):**
- **Slayer** — single-target / execute powerhouse. `Slayer's Dominance` applies
  *Marked for Execution*; the branch leans hard on `Execute` + `Sudden Death`.
- **Colossus** — AoE / Mythic+ powerhouse. Adds the channeled finisher `Demolish`,
  empowered by stacking `Colossal Might`.

**Midnight-new / renamed to flag:**
- **`Master of Warfare`** (apex spec talent, spell 1269314) — **transforms `Slam`
  into `Heroic Strike`** with a stacking Armor Penetration buff. This is why the
  Tier-1 APL emits `heroic_strike` as a filler rather than `slam` in that build.
- **`Demolish`** (spell 436358) and **`Champion's Spear`** (376079) are the
  Colossus / class-tree signature actives current in the Midnight tree.
- Season 1 tier set is **"Rage of the Night Ender"** (2pc: +5% `Mortal Strike` &
  `Cleave` damage, +5% damage taken on the `Colossus Smash` target; 4pc: hitting
  3+ targets with `Mortal Strike`/`Cleave` during the window extends `Colossus
  Smash`). @verify-ingame (tier-set exact numbers from method.gg summary, not yet
  cross-checked vs Blizzard tooltip)
