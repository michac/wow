# Voidforge (12.0.7)

Two-part system from **Decimus**, added in 12.0.5.

## Part 1 — Nebulous Void Cores (bonus rolls)

- **Unlock**: complete Decimus's 6-quest Voidforge questline.
- Then weekly: exchange **gold, Voidlight Marl, or Veteran Dawncrests**
  for **2 Nebulous Void Cores**.
- A core = bonus roll on eligible content:
  - **Delve completion / Prey hunt** → hero-track piece (the only solo uses)
  - **M+10 or higher end-of-run** → **myth-track piece** (best value:
    2 cores + 1 vault = up to 3 myth pieces/week)
  - Raid bosses (TODO: confirm tracks per difficulty)

## Part 2 — Ascendant Voidcores (weapon/trinket overcap)

- **Unlock**: warband-wide **Ascendant Nilhammer** questline from
  Decimus. **Starts**: after the Voidforge is built, Decimus offers
  **"New Tools, New Heights"** at **Howling Ridge, Voidstorm (~51.2,
  68.4)**. 4-week chain: charge a **Hungering Oblivium** through
  endgame content each week. No Voidcores drop until the chain is done
  (came online ~mid-May 2026; Blizzard News, tier 1).
- Combine **5 Ascendant Voidshards → 1 Ascendant Voidcore**.
- Voidshard sources: raids, M+, **Bountiful Delves, Nightmare Prey Hunts**
  (so solo players have a path).
- A Voidcore raises a **fully-upgraded** weapon or trinket past cap:
  - 6/6 Myth **289 → 298**
  - max-quality spark-crafted **285 → 295**
  - 6/6 Hero also eligible (TODO: resulting ilvl)
- Weapons and trinkets **only**; item must already be maxed.

### 12.0.7 change — crafted eligibility fix

- **12.0.7 hotfix:** fixed eligibility so **pre-12.0.5 crafted weapons and
  trinkets** can now receive an Ascendant Voidcore upgrade (previously they
  were wrongly excluded). (Change Ledger, Tier-1 hotfix distillation.)

## Related — Abundant Harvest (Shards of Dundun)

> The Voidforge bonus-roll/upgrade system is **separate** from the void
> *harvesting* loop below; they share the void theme, not the currency.

- **12.0.7:** Abundant Harvest now lets you harvest with **multiple Shards of
  Dundun at once — 1, 2, 4, or 8** in a single action. The rewarded
  **Unalloyed Abundance scales directly** with the number of Shards consumed
  (8 shards ≈ 8× the single-shard reward).
- **Hotfix (post-12.0.7):** fixed a bug where Abundance could not begin while
  in **Voidstorm**; affected players were **refunded via Unalloyed Abundance**.
- Source: Revelations content update notes (Tier 1) + Icy Veins (Tier 3),
  corroborated against Warcraft Wiki.
