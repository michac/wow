# Void Assaults — Void Incursions & Void Strikes (12.0.7)

Rotating open-world assault content that loops between **Eversong Woods** and
**Zul'Aman**, swapping weekly. Collectively called **Void Assaults**; it is a
repeatable loop that awards **Field Accolades**, gear-related rewards, and
**Great Vault** world-content progress.

## Unlock / gating

Pick up the intro quest from **Ranger Captain Lilatha** in Silvermoon City,
which unlocks the **"Void Strike"** quest. This opens the weekly assault wrapper
for whichever zone is active that week.

## The two activities

- **Void Strikes** — the foundational, rotating objectives. Only one Strike is
  active at a time across the zone; completing it spawns another elsewhere,
  forming a chain. Each Strike fills a shared progress bar.
- **Void Incursions** — the larger payoff phase, triggered once enough Strikes
  fill the bar. Structured: ~2-minute prep window → enemy waves → support
  objectives → final boss (Void Imperator).

## Rewards & currencies

- **Field Accolades** — the primary event currency, from Field Pouches (Strikes)
  and larger Field Satchels (Incursions).
- **Dark Particles** — farmed during the loop; spend at vendors (below).
- **Bulging Satchels** — chance to drop on completing Strikes / Incursions;
  contain cosmetics.
- Strike pouches also include **Voidlight Marl** and gold.
- Contributes to the Great Vault **world** slot.

### Vendors (Silvermoon City)

- **Trima Dawnsetter** — sells cosmetic pouches matching **Bulging Satchel**
  contents (including cosmetics from Ritual Sites end chests) for **150 Dark
  Particles** each. *(New in 12.0.7.)*
- **Maren Silverwing** — slot-specific **Heroic** caches (BoP) for **750 Field
  Accolades**; slot-specific **Warbound Champion** caches for **100 Field
  Accolades**.

## 12.0.7 "Revelations" changes (live 2026-06-16)

- **XP from completing a Void Strike or Void Incursion is doubled.** XP is now
  also awarded for **most individual steps** within Strikes/Incursions, to
  balance the ones with more steps. The **weekly wrapper quests** also had their
  XP significantly increased (alongside other similar wrappers).
- **Increased drop rate of Dark Particles** from Strikes and Incursions.
- **Increased drop rate of Bulging Satchels** from Strikes and Incursions.
- **New cosmetic vendor:** Trima Dawnsetter (Dark Particle cosmetic pouches,
  150 each — see above).
