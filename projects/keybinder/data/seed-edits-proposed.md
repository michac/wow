## How build-gating is handled (your follow-up)

The seed is a **single universal default across every build of a spec**. A fix that
binds an ability which only exists under a particular **talent / hero-tree choice**
would put a button on the bar that isn't there for other builds — or rename a slot
that only renames when a talent is taken. So the router is deliberately conservative:

- A finding is sent to **Run past me first** whenever its text references a specific
  build/hero tree/talent (Hellcaller, Diabolist, Void-Scarred, Aldrachi Reaver,
  Dark Ranger, San'layn, Demoniac, "if talented", "the S1 build", "for … builds", …).
  These are tagged **build-gated**.
- All `gap` findings are **Run past me first** by default — they *add* a bind (often
  a talent-specific or transform-only button), which is riskier on a universal seed
  than correcting an existing slot.
- Only **CONFIRMED**, **baseline-true** `wrong`/`stale-name` corrections are
  **Just do it** — a mis-slotted or wrong-spec ability that's wrong for *every* build.

So renamed/talented abilities are **not** silently applied: they surface in the
check-first tier with a `build-gated` tag. Verify each against the live spec before
it ships.
