Soul Shards are the one thing we truly own — readable, branchable, glittering at cap — so they anchor the board. Everything else earns its place: the three borrowed duration bars that tell you how long a window stays open, and a tight ring of the abilities that advance the rotation. Utility is deliberately off the board by default.
